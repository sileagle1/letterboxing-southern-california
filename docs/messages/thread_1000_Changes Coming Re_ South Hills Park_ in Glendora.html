Changes Coming Re: South Hills Park, in Glendora

From: jim54invc (chaosmanor47@gmail.com) | Date: 2013-02-15 23:56:12 UTC
Many thanks for posting this! I suspect that most of the improvements will be on the north side of the park, where there already is a playground area, plus a lot of open space that is under-utilized. I doubt that most of the caches and LBs that are in the park will be affected, as they are mostly higher up, but a couple of them might be affected by a trail that goes all around the park, which I think is a great idea of them to put in. I'll mention this to the people who I know could be affected by this. And anyone who wants to add to the current population of hidden stuff should keep these future changes in mind.

Jim, the other half of chaosmanor
