Sorry Pappa, been down sick with stupid sinus problems - lays me out flat
 
Anyway, my favorite is "Artist's Loft", followed by "Art Performance" - however, these tend to be at least a full inch longer than the "Loft".
 
The other thing I like about the Loft are the caps.  They go on with a definite click that lets me know it is sealed
I know lots of people like La Plume (?)
 
Recently bought some called "American Crafts brand Stamp Marker" got them cheap and they are okay - you get what you pay for :-)
 
Hope that helps - Ratty
