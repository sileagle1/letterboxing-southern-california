South Hills Park -- Up in Smoke!

From: (8hands4jesus@dslextreme.com) | Date: 2013-10-04 07:31:04 UTC-07:00
Several of our Boy Scouts (and parents, myself included) were set up outside of Sam's Club in Glendora on Wednesday, and we saw smoke begin to rise from the nearby hills.  Well, having hiked there as a Troop while earning the Geocaching Merit Badge, we immediately recognized that this new brushfire (it ended up burning 4.2 acres) was in South Hills Park AND on the hill where the Be Prepared! and Come Prepared! letterboxes are/were (as well as a couple of geocaches).  

Yes, well, likely those have also gone up in smoke, as the fire started where the homeless guy (who's been arrested for the fire) had his camp.

We were in a prime spot to watch the whole fire-fighting effort, complete with water-dropping helicopters and airplanes, and they did an outstanding job of knocking out the fire in just a little over 30 minutes.

Our two Scout-themed boxes are surely burned or at least melted, having gone the way of Watership Down (casualty of the Brea Fires, a few years ago).  Once a reasonable amount of time has passed, and assuming the park gets opened up again, for hikers, we'll swing by and assess the damage.

Bye for now...
