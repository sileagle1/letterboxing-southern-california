Hello LB community.  I'm about to take a month off to take my 7 & 10 year old to see some good 'ol USA.  And instead of using my apps, or looking up LB spots, I thought I would first request some ideals boxes/locations.  Maybe because they are yours and could use a wee bit of maintenance.  Or you just want someone else to have an experience as perfect as yours was one day.   See if anything jars your memory, or screams out to you. So here is the route - Leaving Los Angeles and going up PCH.  Stopping to see Elephant seals near San Simeon.  Camping at Julia Pfieffer state park in Big Sur Monday night.  FYI Total Lunar Eclipse Monday night - which will turn the full moon RED.  10pm-1am PST.
Then Monterey Bay; Pacific Grove.  Then Point Reyes - OYSTERS!  Then Petaluma.  Then Santa Rosa.  Then Bodega Bay.  Drop my MIL back off in Santa Rosa. 
Then a meal in Davis.  And Visit a nun in West Sacramento.

I had a few suggestions for the redwoods area, but it looks like most of them have been archived, or are known to have serious problems, so the only one that I can recommend without reservations is "Blue Lake Daffodil":  LbNA # 52529.  For that matter, almost any LB by The Olde Oak is worth a look, as they are often in interesting spots.  You should also give a look to "Avenue of the Giants": LbNA # 36024.  We gave it a try a couple of years ago, but must have misread the instructions, as we never got it, but it's a great walk!

If you have time, give Trillium a look; it's AQ Box #215598.  It is very new, and must be a replacement for an older box in the same general area that we got several years ago, but which is no longer listed.  The Fall Creek unit of Henry Cowell Redwoods SP, northwest of Felton, is less-used than the main section, and tends to be cooler, too.

Ultimately, I suspect that just about any LB that you try for in the redwoods (or almost any other place on your itinerary) will be a marvelous little adventure, just because so much of it is awesome scenery or history!  Have a great trip!  And be sure to let us know which LBs were *your* favorites, when you get home :-D

Jim, the other half of chaosmanor
