I planted this box in August in Rancho Cuyamaca State Park near Julian. I just looked at the official State Park website and it sounds like the whole park is pretty much destroyed so I don't have a lot of hope of the box having survived. :(

The Park is currently closed so there is no way for anyone to get in and confirm whether it is there anymore.

Even sadder than the probable loss of the box is the complete destruction of historical structures that were built in the 30s by the Conservation Corps.
