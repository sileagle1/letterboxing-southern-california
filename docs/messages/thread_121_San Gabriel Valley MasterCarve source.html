From: Dawnette (rbngdfello@aol.com) | Date: 2004-01-05 16:49:00 UTC
Good news for all you San Gabriel Valley residents! My "herd" and I
went to Mrs. Nelson's Bookstore (Bonita X Damien in San Dimas) this
weekend and discovered that they have started carrying MasterCarve.
We bought the last two big blocks they had (4x6", $7.50), but they
still havesets of MasterCarve tools, as well as lots of small blocks
(approx. 2" square) of pre-cut MasterCarve (not sure of the price for
these). This is the only San Gabriel Valley source I know of for
MasterCarve.
