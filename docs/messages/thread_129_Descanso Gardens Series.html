I have received a few e-mails recently about the log books filling up in the Descanso boxes. I went out this weekend and added a second log book to each of the boxes and updated the clues for the #3 Nature Trail box to reflect the bench being moved. I was just in time for box #1 as the lasty page was used on 1/6. I also cleaned some water out of the boxes and replaced all the bags as they all seemed to have been punctured.

When I opened one of the boxes, I found a live pill bug (aka rolly polly bug) in it. I am guessing it got in the day before when the previous visitors were at the box. That is one lucky bug! If I hadn't come out the next day, the next visitors may have found a dead pill bug inside. :)
