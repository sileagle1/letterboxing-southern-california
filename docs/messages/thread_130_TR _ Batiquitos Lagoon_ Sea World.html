From: Spacy Holly (spacyholly@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-01-19 21:01:30 UTC
Batiquitos Lagoon was a great place to visit. The kids didn't even
notice the hills and had a great time reading the log book. The Gecko
stickers were also much appreciated :-) Many geocachers had written
in the log book, so I checked the geocaching website and the box is
listed there too!

For general information, it was a great weekend to visit Sea World, we
were the only RV in the parking lot. We ended up dry camping at
Silver Strand SB on the spit south of Coronado. Since they don't take
reservations, there wasn't a problem getting a parking spot, even
though all the other state beaches along the coast were booked. The
only draw back is that they lock the gate at 7 pm and don't open it
until 8 am.

Happy Hunting,
Spacy Holly
