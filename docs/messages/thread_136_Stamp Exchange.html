Stamp exchange occurs when one letterboxer meets another, you exchange your personal stamp image. Inother words you stamp in their log book and they stamp in yours. If you notice some folks list the number of placed stamps- P 10, number of found stamps -F25, number of exchanged stamps X-5, etc. at the bottom of their posts.
Don P128,F154,X7(I think)
