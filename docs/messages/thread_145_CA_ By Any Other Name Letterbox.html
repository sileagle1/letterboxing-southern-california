From: trentlaudes (trentlaudes@excite.com) | Date: 2004-02-13 07:03:11 UTC
Today my job impelled me to leave the cosy environs of bright and
beautiful Los Angeles County, and venture north towards the orange
groves and coastal fog of beautiful Ventura County. While at work
there, I squeezed in enough time to find Don & Gwen's "By Any Other
Name" letterbox. This is a truly gorgeous stamp, and thanks to my
brand new rainbow set of "Colorbox" stamping inks, I was able to use
the recommended green and red stamps to produce a truly breathtaking
image in my stampbook. Thank you D&G for another fine lesson in stamp
carving.
--Buzzard
