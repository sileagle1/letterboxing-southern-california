Hi All,

I had a chance to do some letterboxing this weekend around San Diego.

The two La Jolla boxes, La Jolla and Tea Time were alive and well.
Both had pretty views of the ocean.

Los Penasquitos Canyon Waterfall Box was alive and well. We picked up
the Arizona Vacation Hitchiker there. A geocacher had also visited,
so bring some stash if you go visit. I didn't have anything suitable
on me to trade :-)

We tried to find The Painted Rocks letterbox (only listed in the
basement), but couldn't come up with it. It's been abandoned for
awhile and very overgrown. We didn't get itchy, but I swear some of
the bushes looked like poison oak. We gave it a good shot, Don!

Webb Park, near Painted Rocks was alive and well.

Sorry Don, we didn't make it to your Jack Creek Falls box - next time!

Happy Hunting,
Spacy Holly
