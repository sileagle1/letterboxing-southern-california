I can talk to Wes about it the next time I see him online and try to
talk him into taking steps to avoid clues being listed on search
engines. But keep in mind, if you have a webpage, there is
absolutely NOTHING to prevent people from linking to it--including
search engines. The major search engines (such as Yahoo or Google)
will honor requests for pages not to be listed, but there's nothing
one can do to prevent it from happening if they really want to go
ahead with it.

Search engines generally find pages from other pages that link to
them. If you want to avoid having them from finding your page,
don't provide link to your clues in the first place. They can't
index it if they can't find it! =)
