From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-04-21 08:42:09 UTC-07:00
If you are traveling up highway 14 you will have some in route; Vasquez Rocks about 1 to 1.5 hrs, Red Rock Canyon (KatPaus) 1 hr, we didn't search for the other one. Burro Schmidt Tunnel would probably take a max of 2.5 to 3 hrs. as the dirt road is slo speed and we put the LB on the other side of the tunnel. You will only be able to go for two of the Coyote Hole Stage Robbery as the Raptor area is closed until July 1. Tubatulabal is right on the way. Fossil Falls is north of Little Lake on highway 14, a driveby, but probably to far out of your way. When you get to Falling Waters ask for the "Old Boy", he is a buddy, and owner. Friday night figure on dinner with us unless you have other plans. We can give you the night time list and send you on your way.
Don
