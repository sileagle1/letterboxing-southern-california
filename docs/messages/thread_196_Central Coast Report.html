From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2004-05-24 00:25:15 UTC
> Did you happen to spot Susie in the bookstore?

You know, it was rather funny, but I went to the bookstore yesterday
and checked up on the boxes there. Imagine when I had seen that
Sierra Sally had logged THAT VERY DAY!!! I asked Susie and Emily
that work there if they caught any 'suspicious characters' lurking
around the photography section, and did remember one person who had
asked about it, but wasn't sure if that was her or not. (They like
to try 'catching' people finding letterboxes in the store, though
they do have a terrible record.)

I wondered around downtown a bit after that checking where
letterboxes were to see if I could track down Sierra Sally, but
alas, it was not meant to be. I warned Suzie and Emily to keep a
lookout for her, though, since she was known to be wandering the
area that afternoon and she didn't get the other letterbox in the
bookstore. (Can't say which one that is--it's a mystery box,
though!) I kind of doubted she'd come back for the second box being
a mystery box and all, but who knows?

In other news, while I was browsing the books myself, Sir B happened
to wander in which was a nice surprise. I hadn't seen him since the
Stamping in Meadow event. =)

Turns out, yesterday was probably the biggest 'gathering' of
letterboxers in SLO since that event--without even trying! Okay,
maybe Suzie and Sir B aren't technically 'letterboxers' anymore, but
still.....

-- Ryan
