From: bilderguys (Bilderguys@socal.rr.com) | Date: 2004-06-03 20:21:00 UTC
We'll after a few weeks of no messages from you guys, I realized that
somehow my yahoo account was "bouncing" you away...oh well...

We had a great camping holiday in the sierra's, and took the
opportunity to try to find a couple of Sierra Sally's boxes. We
enjoyed finding the Dala Horse (what a cute town!) and tried to get
the Fresno Water Tower, but it was closed for Memorial Day. The rest
of the Fresno Cultural Trail will have to wait until our next trip,
but seeing as Grandpa lives in Fresno, it shouldn't be too long! My
husband, who grew up in the area, was familiar with all the box
locations.

We camped about 1 1/2 hours east of Fresno, at Mammoth Pool. The area
is pristine. As beautiful as Yosemite, with no people! We climbed
giant boulders, following Chiquito Creek without a trail for 3 hours
like little mountain goats, until we reached the lake. ( Emma, Claire
and I thought Buzzard would like it) It was closed to boating due to
migrating deer (alas, no swimming deer to be seen) so the place was
empty! We swam, and hiked, and swam and hiked...it was great. And
we managed to avoid all of the thousands of poison oak plants!
(leaves of three, let them be!) If you are in a camping mood, I urge
you to check out this great spot!!
