Periwinkle,
I was very proud to get the "First Finder" certificate for "The Swan"
letterbox this morning. I applaud you for using a true cryptogram
for your letterbox clues--it took me two solid hours to decipher it,
and after that much effort, I was very gung-ho on being the first
stamp. I was questioned by security at the entrance to this
location, but I had already thought up a plausible excuse, and was
given a map of the area that made my quest all the easier (it was
their only map, laminated, and I was told to return it upon
leaving). Thanks for another beautiful stamp in my book (along with
the "Three Birds of Kells" stamps).
--Buzzard
