I got myself all confused about the Squirrel-ambra letterbox clues,
and made a couple of unsuccessful forays into random Pasadena parks,
until I realized the clue was in the letterbox name. Once I figured
that out, I found the LB, and found that the park was really cool.

While being the 1st Stamp in this box as it lay, I understand it is a
replacement, so who knows who found it before. The same cannot be
said of Claire's "Play Tennis" LB, for which I was definitely 1st
finder. Thanks Claire for two great letterbox stamps! This second
stamp revealed to me that the Arroyo Seco channel is open to hikers,
which I never before knew. I think next weekend I'll have my wife
drop me off at this park, and I'll walk up the channel to Pasadena
sounds like great fun! By the way, what did Emma ever do with
my "Horus" Hitchhiker letterbox, does she still have it, or has it
been passed on?
--Buzzard
