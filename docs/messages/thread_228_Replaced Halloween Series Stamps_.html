Replaced Halloween Series Stamps.

From: trentlaudes (trentlaudes@excite.com) | Date: 2004-07-05 22:43:32 UTC
Today I replaced my Rialto Theater, Memorial Park Moon, and Charmed
Witch stamps, so I believe my 6-stamp Halloween Series is again
complete. The Suicide Bridge stamp survived the film crew that
swarmed around it a couple of weeks ago; maybe they found it and left
a note, I'll have to check. I just saw it was there by driving by.
The beauty of this location is there is no stopping on the bridge, so
even though you can see it from your car if you happen to be looking
in the right direction, you can't stop to check it out.

The Charmed Witch letterbox I replaced today features a completely
different carving (better, I think), and is hidden in a completely
different location, so by virtue of the mutable laws of letterboxing
I declare that it counts as a new stamp find for those that found the
original.
