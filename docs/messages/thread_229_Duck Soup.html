I wondered as we enjoyed a pleasant walk through the San Joaquin
Wildlife Sanctuary, "What sort of image would one find on a 'Duck Soup'
stamp?" I laughed when we found it--it's perfect! Thanks California
Bear!

I have driven by this area many times without realizing how extensive
it is. We had the trails almost all to ourselves, which is refreshing
in the middle of O.C. Egrets, herons, avocets, and of course ducks were
plentiful in Ponds B & C. Definitely include this letterbox in your
itinerary when you visit Orange County.
