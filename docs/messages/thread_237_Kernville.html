Kernville

From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-01-07 08:50:57 UTC-08:00
For those that have come up here to letterbox, this would not be the time to return. This morning Gwen & I woke up to about 6 inches of snow, and since then the intensity has increased. I had to suffer, as I had to step gingerly out our bedroom door this morning to reach the spa. There is something really cool about drinking a cup of coffee with the white stuff floating down, and steam rising all about.
I thought that anyone wishing to come up for a few letterboxes should put February 18-21 on their calendar. It will be Whiskey Flat weekend and reservations are necessary for the motels, but there are many activities for non letterboxers. Art shows, western exhibits, gunmen having mock gunfights in the streets,rodeo, all sorts of events. Normally we try to hibernate in our little hillside abode, with so many folks in town, but it is great fun for those coming into Kernville. You would not be able to reach our high country letterboxes, but there is enough letterboxes available to keep you busy all weekend. Another date to watch for is September 23-26, 11th Annual Turkey Vulture Festival.
Don
