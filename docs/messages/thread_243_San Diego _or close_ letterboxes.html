Hello Aj and Munchkins,

We visited a few San Diego Area boxes this spring. If you have a car
they would be fun for kids...

Batiquitos Lagoon - the farthest north, close to the freeway, but fun
for kids

Webb Park - more inland, easy for kids, close to the freeway.

Los Penasquitos Canyon Waterfall Letterbox - inland, this was a longer
hike, but flat. It was hot the day we went.

Tea Time Letterbox
La Jolla - these two La Jolla boxes are easy and probably your best
bet, right on the bluffs overlooking the ocean.

The Hawk Box is new, maybe this summer we'll get to look for it!

Have a great vacation!
Spacy Holly
