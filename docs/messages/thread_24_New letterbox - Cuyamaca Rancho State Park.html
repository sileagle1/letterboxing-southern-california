New letterbox - Cuyamaca Rancho State Park

From: (californiabear@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-08-25 22:02:07 UTC
I planted a new box in Cuyamaca Rancho State Park east of San Diego near Julian.
There is a special bonus for the first finder! My son left a 14-band radio
somewhere near the box. : ( I now own a 14-channel paperweight since I no
longer have the other radio.

I also didn't stamp my log book with my own stamp! It was not the best weekend
for me.

In any event, the area is very beautiful, even in August in the middle of a
drought. If you read the clues, you may notice that there are two ways to get
to the box (fireroad or trail). Even though the fireroad is a little shorter, I
recommend that you take the trail at least one way. It has some really
beautiful views, especially in late afternoon. There is even one spot where you
can see the ocean between two peaks.
