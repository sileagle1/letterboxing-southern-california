Highway 1 road trip

From: jamnjelly033 (jamnjelly033@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-30 04:49:23 UTC
Just a quick update on finds and misses on our Highway 1 road trip.

Mission Santa Barbara, by Sierra Sally: we were first finders on this
box! Logbook was soaked so we dried it out and bagged the logbook.
Great stamp!
Mission La Purisima Conception, by Sierra Sally: First finders
again great stamp and found the Unicycle HH.
Ocean"o", by Judy from AG, Sleepy Cat and Princess Lea: Found in
great shape!
Shady Coyote, by Don and Gwen: Found in great shape!
BMW OH-2, by Maddogbmw: Found also in great shape! Cool stamp but we
drove our Mini Cooper to get to it.
The Bookstore Box, by Ryan Carpenter: Alive and well, had to buy
books to cover our actions! Fun box!
Fluttering Kings, by jennby: we had a tough time finding this one,
but we did. Box is in great shape.
Santa Ines Mission, by Sierra Sally: Again first find, this is a
must series we enjoyed the ones we found and the missions. Thanks
Sierra Sally for the fun.
Drove to the Nojoque Falls and the Turtle box by Don and Gwen: but
found The Solvang Letterbox by Amanda from Seattle's box. Not sure
what happened with the Turtle?
We attempted the Los Osos Oaks series. We didn't find the first box
and due to use both wearing shorts and all the poison oak around we
gave up. Next trip!
Painted Cave, by Don and Gwen: we could not find this one, not sure
if it is missing or we just missed on the clues. The cave and the
drive were great!
We wish we had had more time to find more boxes in the central coast.
We did plant a box in Pismo Beach, look for the clues soon.

Thanks again, Jam n' Jelly
