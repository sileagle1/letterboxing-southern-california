I didn't think your first post sounded snotty. You voiced legitimate
concerns about the way some folks fail to preserve boxes for FUTURE
newbies to find.

I'm suspicious, though, that many of the boxes that get left out in the
open or lost are, in fact, found accidently by noxers (muggles,
mundanes, non-letterboxers, or whatever nomenclature you prefer). Every
time I find a letterbox in a planter (or any other foliage that is
maintained by human hands), I marvel that a gardener hasn't thrown it
away months earlier.
