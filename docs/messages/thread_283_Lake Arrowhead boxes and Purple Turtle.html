Lake Arrowhead boxes and Purple Turtle

From: moderndulcinea (moderndulcinea@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-22 00:10:58 UTC
Hey all! I had a good day at Lake Arrowhead, retrieving 6 of the 7
boxes I searched for, so I thought I'd give an update in case anyone
else wants to head that way. In the order that I found them:

- Mormon Lumber Road.
Looking good!

- Rim of the World Drive.
Everything at this turn-off is under construction EXCEPT for the one
wall where the box is! :-) Hopefully that means the wall isn't
slated for construction. Otherwise, if you haven't picked up this
fabulous stamp yet (I love Chowder's stamps) this might be your last
opportunity, depending on what happens with that wall.

- Crest Park Picnic Area
Closed to the public! (ahem) So I just sort of....got there
early....pulled off to the side of the road...and darted down the
path towards the picnic area. Fire has destroyed a good chunk of
this park and they are in the process of bringing down trees. The
Rotary thing in the clue is still there, and the tree where the
letterbox is hidden is also still standing. However, that particular
tree was tied with blue plastic tape...I don't know if that means it
is being slated for tear down or being saved. The box was still
there and in good condition, but it's hard to say if it will stay
there because of the state of the place.

- Rock Camp
Looking good! And guess what? *****This is where Purple Turtle, one
of the hitchhiking critters from Q's race to NY, stopped!*****

- Grass Valley Lake
Looking good!

-Tunnel II Trail
The only one I gave up on. And it's so frustrating because I know
it's there....it was just replaced this month. But the area is just
a mess, since they're removing trees, and I couldn't tell what was a
footpath from a tractor path from a tree clearing. I was also
concerned about getting lost. So no Tunnel Trail box for me. :-(

- Dragon Woods. New and alive. My last find of the day. Yay!
