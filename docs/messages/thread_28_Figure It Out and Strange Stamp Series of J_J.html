This morning I took my wife on her 3rd letterboxing trip in search of
the "FIGure It Out," and 2nd & 3rd box of Jam & Jelly's "Strange
Stamp Series" (I'd already gotten the 1st stamp in this series).
Although we greatly enjoyed the trip to this location in Santa Fe
Springs, we didn't find the 2nd stamp of the Strange Stamp Series.
This LB is located in a particular tree that has a very deep cavity
for secreting letterboxes, and this morning I just wasn't in the
frame of mind to reach my arm three feet down into a spider web
infested gaping hole to fish around for the box. It didn't seem to
be located in any friendlier locations behind this tree, so if it's
still there, I assume it's deep in the pit. Don't get me wrong, it's
a fantastic hiding place, but for some reason I was squeamish this
morning. If J&J would be willing to confirm for me that this box
requires deep diving, I'd be happy to go back and take the plunge.
(This box is located very close to Southern California Edison's
Whittier Service Center, which I sometimes find myself working at, so
it'd be an easy lunchtime grab.) We were successful in finding the
3rd stamp in the series, and were thrilled to be the 1st stampers!
As usual, a beautifully carved stamp by the Jam & Jelly team. After
this find, we drove to the location of the "FIGure It Out" mystery
box. This box is only a couple of days old, but we didn't find it,
and believe it to be missing. I even searched through the park's
trash cans thinking that some caretaker had found it, and tossed it
thinking it was garbage. All I found in the trash was a Playboy
centerfold, hey, I mean I FOUND A PLAYBOY CENTERFOLD!! At any rate,
maybe Purple Iris, who I believe lives in the area, will confirm if
this box is missing, or we're just lousy searchers. Still, the total
was one beautifully carved stamp image outta three attempts, and a
great day boxing early in the morning in SoCal. We were very happy,
thanks to all!
--Buzzard P6 F58 X-will be "1" after my wife finally carves her
letterboxing signature stamp!!
