Yesterday I planted a letterbox at the Tule Elk Reserve near
Bakersfield. For those heading north to the SLO or SF gatherings it
is a quickie. Picnic tables, shade trees and guaranteed Elk viewing
all can be found at this little oasis. It is just minutes off I-5 and
the Elk are truly fantastic.The Elk from this reserve have been
distributed at various herds throughout California. There are several
wild herds in Lone Pine to Bishop area as well as the Carrizo Plain
and Grizzly Island areas. Look under "Tule Elk" in section 5 on the
map for the letterbox listing. Don
