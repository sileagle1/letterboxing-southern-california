This last weekend I managed to finally replace box #2 in the Seal Beach Town Tour. For those that are quite sick of reading plaques you will be pleased that the replacement has no such requirements. Now on the other hand I couldn't let an opportunity pass, so The Saint Paddy's Day Pub Crawl was also placed. This new letterbox has no plaque reading requirements, but you will have to find your way about, and decipher clues from the signs on the local pubs. I suggest that sampling be put off until you are sure that you have all of the signs written down correctly. Cheers.
