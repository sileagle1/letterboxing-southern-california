That depends where you are going within the state. California has probably the most diverse weather, from seashore to the sierras to deserts.

2. What areas/boxes would you recommend visiting? We prefer hiking
on trails in natural areas when possible. It looks like the San Luis
Obispo area might be good. Comments?

SLO has some great letterboxes and the hikes are very excellant. I think the Montana de Oro area is probably one of the most spectacular seashores I have ever seen. Some of the interior area hikes would be perfect this time of year. The consentration of letterboxes is pretty good as well.

We have a pretty good consentration of letterboxes here in Kernville as well, and the higher altitude letterboxes should still be available. Weather wise- we aren't sure what the heck rain is. I'm hoping that would change and we get a little more water this year. Usually our first storms don't come in until Thanksgiving or later.
You might want to contact Pete & Wanda, Silent Doug, Ryan & Amanda and they might give you an outsider's point of view regarding hikes, letterboxes, and such as they have been in most of the letterboxing areas in Ca.

Hope this helps.
