in Ventura, Sanata Barbara, and San Luis Obispo counties. Of course
we also plan to visit the various wineries in the central coast area.

for boxes in this area. We're looking at the letterboxing.org and
atlasquest sites for clues. Are there any other personal sites that
have additional clues for boxes in this area?

mid-November, but let us know if anyone would like to meet for an
informal exchange somewhere. We'd also love to get any personal
recommendations for restaurants, wineries or hikes in this area. You
can contact me off-list at kotlarek at wi.rr.com.

If bad weather, fires or earthquakes occur, we will scrap this plan
and head a different direction!

Sounds like a good letterboxing vacation. Our letterbox in Ventura, "By Any Other Name" is missing as is our "Painted Cave" in Santa Barbara. If you want to spend most of one day exploring off shore you could try our "Two Too Simple". We do have a combination letterbox with Amanda at Nojoque Falls that should be close to your route. Ron's Ennisbrook Series is a nice walk and has a series signature stamp by a fairly mysterious letterboxer. You will find that with the number of letterboxes in the SLO area you will probably run out of time there.
