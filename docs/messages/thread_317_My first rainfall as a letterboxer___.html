My first rainfall as a letterboxer...

From: Q (puppygirl13@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2004-10-25 03:16:43 UTC

I was surprised to find that all of my letterboxes in Bronson Canyon
survived the storms with the exception of one. And that one was
planted in a dry creekbed (which is not so dry in torrential rain),
so can you blame it? Fortunately, the stamp survived but the logbook
and box were nothing but mud.

Therefore, The Data Recovery Mission #1 is recuperating at home and I
will replace it as soon as I can. It's pretty pointless to go after
any of the others in the series, as this one is the reference point
for the others to make the full picture.

Also, I planted two new letterboxes in the same area. The series is
called To the Batcave!
