Three New Halloween Series Letterboxes Planted.

From: trentlaudes (trentlaudes@excite.com) | Date: 2004-10-31 02:18:05 UTC

I just planted three new Halloween Series letterboxes in Pasadena.
One is a re-plant of the "Scaredy Cat" letterbox, but with an
entirely new (and better) carving in a new location, so that makes it
a new letterbox. The other two are "Black Widow" and "Candy Corn";
brand new letterboxes in the series.

Being urban letterboxes, this series has taken quite a few hits. Here
is the scorecard:
Number 1, the original Suicide Bridge letterbox is still in place.
Number 2, the Rialto Theater letterbox is missing.
Number 3, the replaced Memorial Park Moon is still in place.
Number 4, as noted above, the Memorial Park Scaredy Cat has just been
replaced with a new carving in a new location.
Number 5, the Charmed Witch was destroyed along with the garden at
Pasadena's City Hall.
Number 6, the original Halloween Series Ghost is still in place,
though I haven't checked it since the recent torrential rains.
Number 7, the new Black Widow Letterbox is in place as of today.
Number 8, the new Candy Corn Letterbox is in place as of today.
--Buzzard
