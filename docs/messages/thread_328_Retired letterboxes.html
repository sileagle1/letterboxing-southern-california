Retired letterboxes

From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-11-18 12:51:31 UTC-08:00
We have just retired the following letterboxes: Millard Falls, Jack Creek Falls, The Palo Verde Letterbox, The Cypress Nature Center, Catch the Castaways Curlew, By Any Other Name, Painted Cave, and Wild Goose in California. Out of state letterboxes retired are: Summer Storms, Page Springs Fish Hatchery, and Boulder River Secret. Their listings can still be found on our Atlas Quest logbook but the clue pages have been pulled.

Don
