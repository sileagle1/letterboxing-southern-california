It was with some interest and sadness we learned that the Wild Goose
has been cooked!

abundance of birds in that area.

Hope to see you up our way sometime! The kids still talk about
Kernville and the raft (and Sierra Sally's and mine almost
unfortunate merging with the river lol!)
