Central Coast Weekend Report

From: Sally (Sierra64@aol.com) | Date: 2004-12-13 06:31:38 UTC

Worked like a crazy woman with a dim flashlight at 8pm but was the
first to find The Little Bastard Thursday night. We must all think
the same cause I keep thinking that I'll put one there too... (As a
cattle person, I hope you all remember to shut the gate on the way
in and on the way out!)

Fixed up the Mission San Luis Obispo stamp so it's usable again

Found The Other Building and December 26th in SLO and visited
Jugglermouse too (The Other Building is very cool!) FINALLY found
the Walking Tour stamp after 4 trips to SLO! Good one Ryan! That one
made us work!

Found Box one of St. Patricks Flyer but darkness prevented us from
getting box two or Flutter.

Found Can o Spam in plain sight from our car... tried to hide it a
little better.

Looked for Nature Box in Avila but couldn't find the trail..

Ryan.. the stamp is missing from the bookstore box. Logbook is there
but no stamp.

Had a great time.... Thanks to everybody who planted!

Sierra Sally and Mutau Paul.
