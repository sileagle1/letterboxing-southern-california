Hi All,

Target in Pasadena, near Madre, had two sizes of hand made, unlined
books in their 1 dollar section. The larger is 7x5" for $1 and the
smaller is 3 1/2 x 4", 2 for a $1. The covers are colored heavy
cardstock and the pages are a nice weight white paper.

They also had aluminum, telescoping walking sticks on sale for $5.
Great for the kids...

Should I post this kind of information on the new BoxHeads List
instead of here?
