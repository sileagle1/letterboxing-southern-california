Letterboxing with Autumn

From: (californiabear@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-09-05 15:54:59 UTC
Yesterday, I took Autumn from Colorado for a letterboxing excursion SoCal style.
She was stunned by the density and ease of the two series that we retrieved.
She used to boxes that are hours apart and require hiking to get to. Her
husband called while we were stamping into one of the boxes and she excitedly
told him that she was WALKING, not hiking to boxes and was sitting at a table
while stamping in.

We found all three of the Strange Stamp series and 6 of the 7 El Dorado Park
series (the Nature Center was closed by the time we got over that way). We also
dropped off a hitchhiker in one of the nine boxes we found. :)

I have to say if is kind of hard to be subtle and inconspicous while retrieving
Strange Stamp #2 but it is definately worth it!
