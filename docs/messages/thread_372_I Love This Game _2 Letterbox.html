As with Adventure Seeker, the rain didn't deter me as I took off this
morning in search of the "I Love This Game #2" letterbox planted by 3
Dum Blondes. In the search area, I had to wade through some minor
flooding--enough to make me wonder if I should've opted for the
waterproof hiking boots, even though the salesperson assured me they
really weren't necessary in "arid" SoCal.

Well, they're nice enough hiking boots just the same, though I had to
skip and jump around the deeper water. The important thing is that I
was the first finder of the second 3 Dum Blondes' letterbox, and
received a nice first finder bonus. Thanks 3DBs! Since it was rainy
and cold, I didn't have to worry about anyone being around to spot me
re-hiding your LB. You know, for some time my wife and I have
pondered the idea of moving to Toronto.
