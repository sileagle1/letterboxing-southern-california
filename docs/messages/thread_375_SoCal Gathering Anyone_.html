SoCal Gathering Anyone?

From: Q (puppygirl13@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-02-23 17:53:18 UTC

Is anyone interested in a very low-key SoCal gathering? I have a
Thumbin Critters race-related prize to pass around to a few people
and thought it would be more fun to get together than mail it. Anyone
else want to join in? I have never been to a gathering and have only
ever met one other letterboxer, and would like to be able to put
faces with trail names...

Here are some thoughts:

Who: Everyone...

What: A gathering geared toward exchanges and meeting fellow
SoCal 'boxers

Where: A picnic-friendly park in LA or Pasadena, or even along the
coast/beach. Maybe one with no letterboxes, we plant a few for the
gathering.

When: 1) Any weekend in April (except the 30th-Gold Rush Romp),
2) One of the last two weekends in May, 3) One of the first couple of
weekends in June. A few hours on a Saturday or Sunday.

The date will be determined by the responses I get, so please let me
know when is best for you if you want to attend. Reply to this post
or you can email me at hitchhikerrace at sbcglobal dot net.

Thanks! -Q
