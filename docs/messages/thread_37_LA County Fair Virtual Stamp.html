LA County Fair Virtual Stamp

From: Gnosis 4-H Club (gnosis4-h@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2003-09-10 23:49:15 UTC-07:00
Today I had the opportunity to check on the placement of the letterboxing
display one of our club members entered into the LA County fair, which
starts this Friday in Pomona and runs for the next two weeks/three weekends.

The display is in building 22, tucked away with the other educational
displays and you can see the clues to the virtual letterbox on it. If anyone
manages to get the stamp, be sure and email us so I can pass it along to the
young man who created the display.

He won a first place as well for the display :)
