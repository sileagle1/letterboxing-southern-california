Saturday Letterboxing

From: leenancytanney (luckielee@aol.com) | Date: 2005-02-27 06:24:58 UTC

Did I stutter on my last post, or am I just repeating myself?

Nancy & I had a nice lesurly Saturday of Letterboxing. We spent the
first half of the day maintaining the Hutington Beach Micros, and
added one to it. Then we headed out to Seal Beach and
found "Stingray Bay", "Saint Paddy's Day Pub Crawl (both boxes)", and
at dusk we were able to get "Surf Fishing". This last one drew some
interest from Naval Security. We were monitored by a patrol boat,
but was able to stamp in and get out of there just before Security
Vehicles arrived. If you go after this one, you might want to NOT
carry a backpack. It must have looked awfully suspicious openening
all those containers and re-planting the box. Don - what were you
thinking when you planted this one? As always we loved the clues.

Lee & Nancy

may come back to the area because there is that wonderfully great

fence---BUT if they brought out the dogs my scent was there.

brig for espionage. And it always make for a great letterboxing
