Here is a small mystery for those folks in the San Diego area. There is what we call the "Letterbox Basement" and it was the dumping ground for unclaimed letterboxes when the LBNA site/clues procedure was changed. In Rancho Bernardo a letterbox called "Painted Rocks" went un adopted and the clues are listed in the basement. See post # 37. http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-basement I'm not sure the letterbox still exists, but it is a cool pictoglyph site. If you go make sure you take your binoculars as the box is a short ways away from the pictos. If the box is still in place it needs someone to adopt it.
