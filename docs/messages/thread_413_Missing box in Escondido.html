Missing box in Escondido

From: Cattail (MLK) (marcikoski@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-05-04 05:36:57 UTC
Hey all -

Just a quick note for those planning on traveling to Escondido to do
some letterboxing - the "Christmas Vacation" letterbox is missing,
and I've emailed the planters to let them know. I was easily able
to find the location of the box, but all that was left was the lid!
I hunted around quite a bit to see if I could find the logbook or
stamp, but to no avail. Bummer!

On a happier note, I was able to find the "Painted Rocks" box near
Lake Hodges in Rancho Bernardo. It was perched right on top of some
rocks, not hidden, and in plain view of the trail. This box is
listed in the letterbox basement, and I was glad that someone had
taken the time to give the box some TLC, but it appeared that the
last entry (before me the other day) was in 2003! I thought that
some people had visited since then, but perhaps not. It was a very
nice hike - the waterfall was running, and the day was beautiful,
and the box has been rehidden.

Have a good rest of your week!
Cattail :)
