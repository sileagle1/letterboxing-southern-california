alarch2 wrote:
I retired my Marvys from letterboxing after seeing what happens to
water-soluble stamps in wet logbooks! I love brush pens, but it's been
hard to find a waterproof substitute. Pens are a bit tricky, too. You
can get streaks inking large areas, and if you use several on one
stamp the first ones can dry while you're adding the others. I also
bought one of the "option" pads with the 12 lift-out sections. It's
awkward compared to pens, but the colors are cool and the coverage is
pretty consistant. It was abt. $18.00. Call around, or check the web,
because it comes in different color themes- at that price, you should
have a choice! :)
-Alarch
