Old Zoo Gathering Update

From: Q (puppygirl13@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-05-18 02:52:57 UTC
Hey Gang! Just a friendly reminder: The Old Zoo Gathering is less
than a week away! There are 15 letterboxes waiting to be hunted and
the clues will be available the day of. (Yes, I've been busy...)

The first seven are the Old Zoo series which are really close
together and hopefully won't take more than an hour or so to hunt.
This series is geared toward kids and those who might not be up for a
hike. This series will be pulled after the gathering - the Old Zoo
picnic area is always pretty busy and I doubt they would survive long
anyway.

The next series is a surprise, but is geared toward those who DO want
to hike. I have hiked every trail leading out from the Old Zoo to the
top of Mt. Hollywood and found the easiest, which is still a moderate
loop that takes an hour and a half without stopping to find a bunch
of letterboxes along the way. I figure a good time to hunt these is
after lunch, when it's nice and hot :)

Also, I hate to exclude anyone from letterboxes, but just so everyone
is aware, there are two letterboxes that will be available only to a
select few who took place in the Thumbin' Critters Hitchhiker Race.
Sorry!

I strongly suggest everyone bring: lunch, lots of water, sunscreen,
bug repellent (though they aren't too bad yet), ink pads (none of my
boxes will have them), and comfy shoes!

Ok, and now for the bad news. There is a very real possibility that I
will have to work on the day of my own gathering. :( I won't bore you
to death with the nuances of my job, but when there's work I have to
take it, and I'm pretty sure there will be. If so, my boyfriend has
volunteered to go early and save some picnic tables until the
majority of the group gets there. He is not much of a letterboxer, so
he won't stay the entire time, but at least long enough to hand over
the event box and race boxes to someone responsible. I will try my
best to show up at noon for lunch so I can at least meet everyone :)

Anyway, in the next day or so I will post directions to the Old Zoo
picnic area and the exact tables I hope to get, so check back! And I
look forward to seeing everybody on Sunday! -Q
