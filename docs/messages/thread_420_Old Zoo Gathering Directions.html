Directions

There are a million ways to enter Griffith Park, but to get to the
Old Zoo Picnic Area, you need to turn onto Griffith Park Drive from
Crystal Springs Drive, which parallels I-5. There are three exits off
the I-5 that get you to Griffith Park: Los Feliz Blvd., Griffith
Park, and Zoo Dr. In all cases, just follow the signs to the park
they will take you to Crystal Springs Dr. From Los Feliz and Griffith
Park exits, you need to head north on Crystal Springs and turn left
on Griffith Park Drive there are signs that list the Old Zoo area
this way. From Zoo Drive, you need to head south on Crystal Springs
and turn right on Griffith Park Dr. Going south on the I-5, I don't
think there is a Griffith Park exit so take one of the other two.
