Gwen & I had an opportunity to nab Gerwiian's(could be Hawimany?)
first letterbox. Without a doubt this is one of the coolest and
best "first time I'ved carved a stamp" stamps we have ever seen. The
first finder award was pretty remarkable too. A very nice walk on a
warm day that had a good amount of shade, plus a great view of a water
feature up river that totally scares the c--p out of a white water
boater. The restrooms 1/2 way along the route were placed nicely too.
Thanks Gerwiian.

We were going to go for another letterbox in the area that was planted
by another letterboxer, but looked at the entry price of $7.95 per
person and decided we would pass.
