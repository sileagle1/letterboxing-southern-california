> Can anyone give us a heads up about any spectacular or
> recently missing boxes?

I think most of Sir B and JugglerMouse's letterboxes are missing at
this point. There's still a few out there, but your odds of success
are much lower if you hunt down those boxes.

I'm kind of fond of my Los Osos Oaks series as it was the first series
I ever planted. =) They're all alive and well as of the last time I
checked. The Walking Tour and Bookstore Box are also doing well at
last check. (Well, the Bookstore Box's stamp is broken in two, but
it's still there and findable.)

Wish I were in the area to show you around a bit, but alas, you must
enjoy the trails without me. =)

Happy trails!

-- Ryan
