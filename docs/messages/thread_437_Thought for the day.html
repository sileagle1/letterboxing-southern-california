From: Robyn (Elite2713@aol.com) | Date: 2005-08-12 11:12:30 UTC
While on vacation on the Central Coast of California this past July
we were letterboxing every chance we could until we ran into a rattle
snake. We then decided to wait until it cooled down. What we were
also finding is that the clues would tell us it's "only a 1/4 mile
hike or a 5 minute stroll" then after a mile down the trail we'd find
the right spot but no letterbox and when you top that off with 90 to
100 degree heat, it's very discouraging.
Can't wait for the fall and cooler weather.
