Subject: Road Trip

Hi Folks,
Gwen & I are back in Kernville from our "Find the cooler weather road
trip". We did find some cooler weather as well as a few letterboxes. I
want to appologize to anyone that tried to send us email. Just before
we left I cranked our spam protection up to max and that created a few
bounced messages. One thing that was curious though was that
the "contact the placer function" got through the barrier and we did
receive updates on several of our letterboxes. Not only did we find
several letterboxes but left a few in our wake and anyone traveling up
395 should check the gold country area for a few more. We did replace
the Fossil Falls letterbox near Little Lake and it has a new location
and a new stamp. Lee and Nancy should love this image and since both
the stamp is new and the location at the Falls is new we have added
this one to our placed count and we now have a 200 P total. Hope
everyone is having a good summer.
Don
