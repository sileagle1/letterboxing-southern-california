Re: [letterboxing-socal] Re: Shorebird

From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-05-03 08:48:26 UTC-07:00
Yes I think Signal Hill is the oldest in the LA area. I did get an 'attempted' report on it though, so who knows if it is still there. #15 and #16 in the state reside here in Kernville along with some other older boxes. For those folks that like White Water Rafting, the upper Kern is at 3000 CFS and will get bigger!!!!!!!!!! If anyone does come up here to do boating, make sure it is with a licensed outfitter. You can check with the Forest Service for outfitter info. Once you book and get up here then you should try to find out which guides on the trip have the most experience and make sure that is the boat you get into. This year first and second year guides might have a few problems.

Don
