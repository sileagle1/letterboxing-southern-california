From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-01-21 17:04:21 UTC

Welcome to Lost Lenore and Uberwalder.It looks like the SoCal group
has two more new addicted members.

We received a note from another letterboxer this morning advising
that our 'Real 49er' at LB State had some weird code written in the
log as well as some obcenities and they were reluctant to tear out
the pages of the logbook. Gwen and I feel that once we place a
letterbox it becomes a public(letterboxing) property and if any
finder sees that it needs some maintenance please feel as though you
can deal with it. We always take additional supplies with us when we
are out searching for boxes and everyone else might want to consider
doing the same. We will be back in the Long Beach area around the
first of Feb and can take care of the problem. We do appreciate the
notice that the letterboxer sent us as well as other notes from
finders.It is always good to hear from finders. It was surprising
that this box was vandalised as it seems that only 1 in 5
letterboxers has been able to find this box.

Still looking for anyone that wants to help out at the Rancho.

BTW we were first finders in 3 of Lee & Nancy's letterboxes in the
Apple Valley area last week. Great stamps! One of the boxes had been
there for over a year. This is the time of year to make a high
desert trip.

Don
