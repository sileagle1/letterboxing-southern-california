From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-01-21 13:07:57 UTC-08:00
Hi Dunegirl

Always think "Stealth". Consider retrieval and rehide of a letterbox as one of the most secretive things you can do.
Here are a few links to some sites that you can peruse and gain some knowledge on Letterboxing.

Don

Letterboxing.org:
http://www.letterboxing.org/faq/faq.html

Silent Doug's site:
http://www.letterboxing.info/
Doug probably has the best info site on Hitchhikers and links to regional chatlists.

AtlasQuest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/
Ryan has a link set up "Code of Conduct" that is really good for all
boxers to read

newboxers' group:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/
Where there is no dumb question, and is "the" place for many
newletterboxers.
