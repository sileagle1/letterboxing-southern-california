Here is some of the things discussed at the meeting in Long Beach.
In attendance were representatives from Rancho Los Alamitos, Rancho
Los Cerritos, and Long Beach Museum of Art. Gwen & I represented the
letterboxing community. A fairly in depth description of
letterboxing was given, as well as a discussion on how the
letterboxing could be used for a special event with temp boxes and
how a permanent box could be set up at both Ranchos. The Art Museum
has different goals, and they may not want to participate. We left
some lockable metal boxes for both Ranchos as they will eventually
will want a permanent on site letterbox. The lockable boxes will
help eliminate vandalism, and access will be provided through the
clues(combo locks). Their concept of having a gathering in May has
been put off until October. At this gathering they would like
several temp boxes with clues handed out and the general public
invited. We realize and advised that this tended to be opposite of
what we (letterboxers) generally think of as a gathering due to the
stealth aspect of LB, but felt that with carving clinics, clue
writing clinics and other informational areas it still would be a
worthwhile project. We did advise how we generally shun publicity,
but even though they want publicity it could work. The only clue
sheets that would be handed out would be for those temps for the
Museums or Ranchos. Anyone wanting to look for more letterboxes
would have to go on line and find them there. This would help with
newboxers having a negative impact on those letterboxes currently
planted in the surrounding area. The Representatives will be getting
back to us and we will report on this as things develop.

Don & Gwen
