I'm still alive...

From: Eric Polk (californiabear@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-02-17 18:37:50 UTC
Hi all! It's been a while since I have been around here and I see
that you have all been busy what with the Rancho thing, a bunch of new
users and a ton of new boxes!

As for me, I have a new baby in my house as of October so that has
been keeping me really busy. My apologies to anyone who has tried to
contact me via e-mail that I did not respond to. Between being busy
with the baby, all the spam and all the messages from the letterbox
groups, I'm sure some have been lost in the shuffle.

I have a little bit more time for some letterboxing now and I took a
chance this morning to enter "The Bermuda Triangle" in Cerritos. It
feels good to be back in action!

The next thing I need to do is to check up on all of my boxes and see
how they are doing. I have a couple of missing box reports filed away
somewhere in my e-mail inbox that I need to follow up on.

As for the Rancho letterboxing event, I will probably not be in a
position to coordinate the event but I can help out with a workshop on
something. I am thinking maybe clue writing. I also think that Bob's
(at least I think it was Bob) idea of making stamps out of Fun Foam
shapes cut with scissors and glued to wood blocks might be a good
workshop to do for that event as it will give people who are terrified
of stamp carving a way to make their own signature stamps.

Well, back to the salt mines. I'll try to pay more attention to the
group now and keep up with stuff in the future.

Eric aka California Bear
