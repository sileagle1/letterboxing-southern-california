From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-02-18 17:25:05 UTC
Well the warm temps have changed to cold, and the surrounding hill
sides are blanketed with snow. Tonight we have a 60% chance of snow
down to 1500 feet (Kernville is at 2700 ft). Our little sleepy town of
1600 will be exploding up to 30 or 40 thousand due to our annual
Whiskey Flat celebration. Gwen & I are going to be hunkering down in
our abode awaiting the return of peace and quiet and the arrival of a
celebrated Princess. The real bummer is that our spa is on the fritz.

Don

Flathead. Only two of the boxes need a screwdriver. This was a test on empty paint cans purchased at Home Depot. One thing that I find interesting is the concept of charging us here in the Valley at resort prices for power while generating(industrial) power from a river, that if it were allowed to flow without removal of the water, would bring more people to the resort. Kinda difficult to figure out.
