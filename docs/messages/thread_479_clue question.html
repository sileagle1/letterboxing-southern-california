Clues with degrees refer to compass headings. In some cases, you may
not need the compass as the degrees give a general idea as to the
direction you should go (i.e., follow the trail at 130 degrees).

A rough guide to compass direction is to remember that north is 0
degrees (or 360 degrees since there are 360 degrees in a circle).
East is 90 degrees, south is 180 degrees, and west is 270 degrees.
This may be helpful for the rough directions I mentioned above but it
would be most helpful to have a compass.
