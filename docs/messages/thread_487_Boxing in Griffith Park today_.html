Boxing in Griffith Park today.

From: buzzardpasadena (disbarro@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-04-17 01:10:18 UTC
Got out my newly carved Buzzard stamp replacement and headed off to
Griffith Park today. Found Lee & Nancy's "Feed the Children" box--
really beautiful carving--you'd love it, D&G. It's hidden amongst a
lot of poison oak and stinging nettle, but hey this is Southern
California, what do you expect, tulips? I confirmed that the first box
in my Amir's Garden Series is missing, which had been reported to me.
This box was hidden in tall, thick grass . . . which has since been
completely chopped down to the ground (heavy sigh). I'm happy to
report, however, that Q's LB, hidden nearby, is still there, as is the
second box in my series. The survival of my second box surprises me,
since it is hidden in a garden where they do things like water and
weed; I would have thought they'd have found it for sure. It's had a
lot of visitors. Oh, and I found a watch!
