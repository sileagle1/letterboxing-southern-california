Was Ice House, now Kern

From: Gwen and Don Jackson (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-04-23 09:02:54 UTC-07:00
Right now is the perfect hiking weather, and the Kern is starting to get pretty good as well. This year the upper Kern should be peaking at 6 or 7 thousand CFS. The lake level is up considerably and some predict a spillway party this year. The only time I've seen it go over the spillway was in 1983 and that made the lower unrunnable for commercial trips, but it was a very quick run for those that did get to boat it. It only took about 2 hours on the water to do the regular two day run.

Don
