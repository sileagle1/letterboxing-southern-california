Re: Seal Beach Town Tour

From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-10-14 02:56:54 UTC
For any of you locals that have never done this series, now is your chance to check out this classic set of boxes in a lovely beach community.

As some of you know, Gwen asked me to adopt several of her boxes since she will no longer be in the area as often, having sold her Seal Beach apartment. Some of them are doing just fine, such as the three at CSULB and the Signal Hill box, the oldest active listing in LA County (the current logbooks inside go back to 2007, but the current stamp has been there since 2003).

Several others are missing, some of which I simply retired for various reasons (CJs Funbus is gone, for example). But others I intend to replace, and to that end, I got four planted today: three of the Town Tour and the Signal Hill Races.

Don and Gwen's boxes were a huge influence on me early in my boxing career: of the first 26 boxes I found in So Cal, 21 were theirs. My newest plant in San Bernardino uses the evil "plaque code" I first learned in Seal Beach.

Happy boxing,
Kelsung
