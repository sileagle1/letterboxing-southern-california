Hello,
If you search LBNA, you will find a few of my boxes close by. From
the Devore area where the I-15 and I-215 meet you could go after:

Out of the Park ( 1 box) - 3.5 miles south along I-215 - driveby,

Lost Lake (2 boxes) - 5 miles north - short hike of geologic interest,

Pacific Crest Trail (4 boxes) - 7 miles north - moderate hike,

Cajon Summit (2 boxes) - 12 miles north - short hike


Not many people have hit these, they should all be in place.

Lee & Nancy
