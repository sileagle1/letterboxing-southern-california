For anybody who checked out my first series of six letterboxes, I just
wanted to let you know I've finally replaced "The Pirate's Booty"
after many months. I was concerned the original would come up
missing, since the park had no good hiding places, and sure enough it
did within a few weeks of it's initial placement. After much thought
and procrastination, I finally put a new letterbox out in a different
park. Gone are the semi-clever clues I'd written, but now I'm
confident the box will be there when you go looking.

Of course, I've still got a box missing in my Ocean Blvd Series and
one missing in my San Pedro Series, so I need to get to work.
