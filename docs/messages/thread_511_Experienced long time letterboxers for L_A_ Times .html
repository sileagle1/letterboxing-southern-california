Hello Shana

I am one of the Moderators for this list. Unfortunately I have been
out of town for most of the summer and had switched off my receiving
mail or notices from this group. My apologies for not answering your
post.
While I'm reluctant to do interviews, here is a way to reach the
owner of the list and another Moderator. On the left hand side of
the SoCal home page click on 'members' then you will find a box that
says search members and you should type in "California Bear" and
that will give you a listing for epolk. He is the owner of this
list. He gets all of the communication on a daily digest so he
should be receiving this tonight.
Another Moderator is "rscarpen" and you can get his information the
same way as listed above. Ryan is his first name and he gets no
email from this group as he moderates several other groups and
sites.

You may be wondering about my reluctance for such an interview, but
you may get similar reactions from other "Silverbacks". From our
perspective Letterboxing is best spread by word of mouth. Some new
letterboxers come into our game after reading an article and don't
do sufficient research. They forget about being stealthy and sneaky
when they retrieve a letterbox and many planted boxes go missing.
For example when Time magazine wrote a piece we lost 15 of our
letterboxes the very next week. Now that could have been just a
coincidence, but it is my guess that it was not. Sometimes publicity
isn't a good thing.
There have been lengthy and heated discussions on other chatlists
and boards regarding this very topic as many of us see some of the
nuances of letterboxing getting lost in most of the articles being
written.

Don
