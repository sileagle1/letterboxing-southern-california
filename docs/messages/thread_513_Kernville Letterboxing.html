Kernville Letterboxing

From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-09-16 01:20:28 UTC
Gwen & I spent a really cool day in the high country above Kernville
chasing two of Katpau's letterboxes. We found the 'Hole-in-the-Stump'
LB but had no luck with the 'Oak Flat' LB.
For anyone that has been considerating heading to Kernville
letterboxing, and has been waiting for cooler temps now is the time.
The higher altitudes was hovering in the low 50s today while in
Kernville it was in the high 70s. So you can hit the lower boxes
around town in the morning then head to the high country in the mid
day. There are not as many folks in town these days as compared to
spring and summer.
