Box Nappers?

From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-10-02 20:47:36 UTC
I just returned from Seal Beach and thought I should post a
little 'Heads up' for those that have some boxes planted in the
area. Our Seal Beach Town Tour has taken some hits. #2 is missing,
Red Car was missing but has been replaced, Fish in the Reads is
missing and Anaheim Landing is missing. Just a Number is okay in
it's concrete lump and Grandsma's Seal of Approval is okay as well.

We had received a report about our Signal Hill Views box, "the stamp
was missing". I carved a replacement and left it in the box. I
checked on Signal Hill Races and found the box in place but someone
had left a store bought stamp and new logbook as some previous
finder had taken the logbook and stamp.I pulled the box. The note
inside did say that this was their 2nd letterbox and they found it
empty, but they never contacted us about it.

I check Catching some Sun in the Quad and 49er Mascot, both okay.
One was a little damp.

It may have just been a fluke that so many Seal Beach boxes and
Signal Hill boxes went missing and had stamps stolen but there could
be a new boxer or new geocacher that either isn't being careful or
is taking stuff.

The Family Fun Magazine did run an article about 3 weeks ago, and
there has been a large influx of new letterboxers.

Don
