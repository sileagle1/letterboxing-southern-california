Now if you want to do some research and look for an oldy but mouldy
letterbox that is probably gone you could go back in time on the
main list and look for a plant by Wendy Gault. She planted it around
1999 or 2000? In those days every clue was posted to the main list
and the web masters posted it up on LbNA. (Boy they did all that
work!!!!) I can't recall the name of the box but she didn't have
more than 3 plants. The clue started out "name of the place where
the cubs practiced" or something like it. The plant was in or near
the cemetary and had directions from a specific head stone. Wendy
never confirmed that it was missing. It is not in the basement nor
in old LbNA.

If you could locate that box it would have a super stamp as she was
a very exceptional carver.
