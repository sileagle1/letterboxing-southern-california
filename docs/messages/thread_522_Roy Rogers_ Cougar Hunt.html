Roy Rogers' Cougar Hunt

From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-10-23 23:47:04 UTC
We just spent the day in the high country above Kernville today
checking on our 6 box drive series. The roads are in great shape and
there is only a little bit of the road degraded near the end when you
get closest to the cave, but 2x2 with good clearance should be no
problem. Deep Creek cave looks like it is still accessable as someone
has sawed off the two rebar that blocked the top part of the entrance.
Neither Gwen nor I went in this time due to size requirements (can't
squeeze into tight spots any longer), but the area was very pretty
with the fall colors changing in the high country. Deer season is over
and these roads will be closed by FS sometime around 12/1, so now is
the time for the drive.
