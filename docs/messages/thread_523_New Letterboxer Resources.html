Yep, and you should click on "Links" just above "members", then
click on California State Landmarks and then scroll down to Ca.
Landmarks by county.

These can help you (on some) figure out plaque codes before you
leave home.

Our Seal Beach Town Tour can not be done on your duff, but some
other Town Tours with plaque codes can be figured out before you hit
the trails. A good example is the Coyote Holes Robbery. If you check
the clues then find the plaque on line you can actually get the
clues solved at home (for one box). This is nice as sometimes the
temps at the plaque location can exceed 110.
