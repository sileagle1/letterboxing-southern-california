We lived in Riverside for two decades, although we've been living in
Ventura County for 13 years, so thing have likely changed a lot since
we moved. But there used to be a large stationary store in the south
end of the downtown Mile Square area, a little west of the 91 freeway,
on 14th Street. I can't remember the name, and there is a good chance
that it went out of business if a Staples or Office Depot went in
nearby, but it can't hurt to give a look if you ever go out that way.
There was also a large store in San Bernardino, near the mall, but
that's even chancier, I would imagine.

FWIW, any college or university (including community/city colleges)
which has an art department will carry carving supplies in the
bookstore. All public colleges (Cal State, UC and the CC system) are
open to the public for things such as using the libraries (not for
checking out books, however) as well as buying stuff in their
bookstores. I don't know which CC is closest to you (Citrus? that
seems a bit far), but you probably do :-) The best time for checking
out the bookstores is mid-afternoon, when the class level is lower and
there are more parking spaces open. Just don't expect any bargains
:-o Good luck!

chaosmanor
