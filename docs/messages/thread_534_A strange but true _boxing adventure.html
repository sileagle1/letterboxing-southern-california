A strange but true 'boxing adventure

From: (scoutingbear1978@aol.com) | Date: 2006-11-30 21:06:17 UTC-05:00
This was such as weird and interesting adventure, I just had to share it
with people who would understand!

This past Sunday I set out to check the status of boxes in the City Heroes
series and add a new box to the group. I had received missing reports for 2 of
the boxes and had already recarved these boxes as well. When we arrived, I
looked first for the series stamp, but it was gone. In the meantime, my hiking
buddy found a geocache just sitting out, amongst some rocks in a cameo
container. Being the good boxers we are, we stamped one of the trinkets so at
least we could have a "find" for the day. That being done, I tried one more time
to find my box I had cameo taped and so carefully hid. I look down in teh
bushes and see a plastic container! Not the cameo box I had last hidden, but the
original stamp that had gone missing 4 months ago...

We continue on. The next 2 boxes...missing. But not the two I had been
receiving reports on. Recent fires and trail work may have had something to do
with it as they were planted near trees.

Then, to the next box which I had received many missing, poison oak, etc.
reports about. Not a trace of poison oak or even stalks. And there it was! Just
sitting under its rock, completely undisturbed.

Next box was great, replaced the one after that (knew that one was missing.
It was confirmed falling down a hole). Then, the last box was in a very
burned out area. No trees or shrubs left. I thought for sure this would be gone
when I saw the area....but no! The box had a bit of melted edges but there it
was, just sitting and waiting to be found again!

All said, the city heroes series has plenty of boxes that are in good shape
now and the other 2 shall be replaced soon!
