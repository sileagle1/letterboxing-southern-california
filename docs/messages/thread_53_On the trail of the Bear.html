I actually didn't put any more boxes at Crater Lake although I thought the Pinnacles area would be a cool spot. We took a trail from the Pinnacles parking area and found a big East Entrance sign for Crater Lake NP on the trail. It was really strange to see this huge stone and wood sign next to a single track trail. I think there used to be a road that came up there because we saw a section of woods where all the trees were younger than the others. That section was suspiciously about as wide as a road and ran toward the parking area.

Anyway! I was going to say that would be a great spot for a box because it is only about 1/2 mile trail and technically, the box could be placed outside the border marker of the Park.

The parks and monuments I placed in were further south than Crater Lake. I am still trying to figure out how to word the clues to hide their true location.
