I don't often get to help out on things like this, and I don't have a
terribly-large number of suggestions, but one that I *can* make that
is pretty easy, and not too far off the freeway, is one which my wife
and I did a just a couple-three weeks ago. And we left an HH in it,
too :-) It's Kanan Dume Tunnel #1, which is about six miles south of
the 101. Get off on Kanan Road, and head south. The parking area is
on the right, just before Tunnel #1; you'll drive through tunnels #3
and #2 before that. It's about a 10-15 minute walk to the box, and
there are some nice views from along this trail. Total time off the
freeway is an hour or less. And if you want, you can go down Kanan
Road to Pacific Coast Highway (CA 1), turn right and catch the freeway
just before Oxnard. However, you will want to take Las Posas Road or
Rice Road north, rather than driving through Oxnard!

Hope this helps! HAve a safe trip :-)

Jim (chaosmanor)
