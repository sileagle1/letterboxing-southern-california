You can use the 'Contact the Placer' function at LbNA or a similar
function at AQ. You could also go to the members list on any
chatlist, type in the letterboxers name or trail name in the search
function and write them a note. Another method would be to write a
letterbox report on all of the boxes here (if they are SoCal) and
you can let all of the planters know about all of the boxes.
Every planter that I'm aware of likes personal feed back on their
letterboxes whether they are found, missing or 'can't find'.
