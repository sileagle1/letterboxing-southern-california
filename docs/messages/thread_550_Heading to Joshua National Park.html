Joshua Tree is an area contolled by NPS and it is illegal to plant
boxes in National Parks. That being said if you go to AQ and type in
Joshua Tree in the advanced search you will come up with one
called "Old Time Theater". Others in the area are the boxes in Palm
$pring$. If you plan your trip through Apple Valley you will be able
to go for Lee and Nancy's boxes.They have some in Redlands as well.
Lee is a gifted carver and their boxes are not frequented in these
areas as much as their other boxes.
Check both AQ and LbNA.
