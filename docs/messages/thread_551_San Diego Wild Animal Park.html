From: Lyda Mae Young (lydamae27@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-15 17:18:31 UTC-08:00
Hello Tydans:

Just wanted to let you know that we found the Kit Carson Park LB not too long ago. It was a bit tricky. Here is what you should do:

1) When looking for the "purple Tyler pipe" you will be all the way into the woodsy part of the trail.

2) the broken heart shaped stump in no longer heart shaped (some more has broken off).
It is small. If you stand at the stump and look over your right shoulder you will see the " 3 large trees in a row " If you go the 8 paces from the stump you will only see the first of the 3 trees because they are lined up and it blocks the other two. So, from the broken stump - identify the 3 trees THEN go 8 paces. From that spot you can take the 19 paces off the trail toward the trees.

I hope this helps! The sculpture garden is really COOL!

Lyda
