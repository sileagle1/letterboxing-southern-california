Yeah, I have just got involved with LB'ing due to that article.
However, I have been involved with the international mailart network
for 30 years. Mailart is an activity which connects artists around the
world via the postal system. The internet has now taken the
communication place of what M/A did, but there is still the draw
(especially strong with artists) of getting the actual object in your
mail box, sorta like LB.
I have been carving my own stamps for 25 years, thanks too to M/A, so
LBing was a natural for me. I also bike a lot (100-150 miles per
week), so many of my first attempts have been along my cycling routes
in Huntington Beach, Seal Beach and Fullerton. It's a nice excuse to
take a break on my rides and it will encourage me to develope new bike
routes to get to new LBs in the future.
