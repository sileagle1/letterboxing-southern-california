New San Diego Gathering Location TBD

From: geishagirl29127 (geishagirl29127@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-01-31 04:09:20 UTC
Hi Everyone,

I am so glad that so many of you have signed up for the event
already!! I don't think we've ever had so many people sign up for a
gathering here in Southern California...This is all very exciting!!

Since the number of attendees is steadily growing (we're already at
54 people!!), I am going to look for a more suitable place to
accommodate such a large group, and that allows pets (at least on a
leash), and has a playground for the kids nearby. The location will
still be in the City of San Diego. I have a few parks in mind, and
I'm working to find out the details and regulations of the different
parks. Please bear with me as I search for a new location. I should
have an announcement for the new location in a few weeks.

San Diego locals, if you have any suggestions, please let me know! We
need a place with a lot of grass, at least 3 tables, and if
reservations for a large group is necessary, something that isn't too
pricey to reserve.

We are less than two months away from the event! Thanks everyone for
your feedback so far and your offers of help. They are very much
appreciated!!!

Regards,
GG
