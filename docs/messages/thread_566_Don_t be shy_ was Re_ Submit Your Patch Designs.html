It's been brought to my attention that some people may be avoiding
entering patch designs because of the intimidation of having their work
published on the Pepe's well-known blog, so if those fears do exist, I
want to allay them. The voting page will not be available to the
letterboxing public at large like most of the other pages on the blog.
The link to it will only be made known to the members of the California
Yahoo groups. This will keep the voting from being swayed by people
who aren't really interested in the patches, and apparently it might
also encourage any bashful artists out there as well. ;) So please,
submit away!

And if there are any more comments or questions, please don't hesitate
to ask.
