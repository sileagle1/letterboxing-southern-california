Patch Contest Page is Up!

From: Mark Pepe (mjpepe1@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-02-19 14:03:54 UTC
Hi boxers:

Our page for the new patch contest is ready for your viewing. There
are 7 designs from which you may choose your favorite.

There is a poll on the right hand side of the page where you may cast
your vote for your favorite patch. You may vote once per day. The
patch with the most votes as of midnight Eastern time next Monday,
February 26 will be declared the winner.

Here's the link for the webpage: http://socalpatches.blogspot.com/

It is only published here so only those from the So Cal and No Cal
lists know about this webpage. This will help ensure that those
voting are only from the CA lists.

Thanks for allowing us to help out!

Mark

GeoGeorgia,

Actually, three finds in 10 days isn't bad. It took my first series of
letterboxes THREE weeks to get a single finder. And that was back in
October! And just this week the brave DemXXMen found all three. So
two finds in four months. (One important detail to mention is that the
first box is on top of a mountain and it's about an 8mi roundtrip
hike--hey at least it deters vandals!)

Don't take it personally. I think it's also important to remember that
only ONE weekend has passed since you planted.

Don't lose hope. I'm sure your letterbox will continue to get traffic.
I'm not down that way often, but when I am you can bet I will check it
out. I've now put it on my watch list for AQ.

Hang in there!!

Renee
