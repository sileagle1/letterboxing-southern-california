I finally got around to actually looking at the designs, and there are
some great ones on that page. I could go with any of them, although I
definitely have several favorites. Looking over the voting so far, I
would suggest that any which get less than 10% be dropped, and then we
have a second round. At this point, five of the designs would be
dropped. Three of the top four are all very close, and the fourth is
just barely hanging in there, but every contest should have a "dark
horse". In the second round, if no design wins by a 10% margin, then
we should take the top two and have one final round. Just my two
cents :-)
