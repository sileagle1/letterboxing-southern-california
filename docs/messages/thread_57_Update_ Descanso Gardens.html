Update: Descanso Gardens

From: Spacy Holly (spacyholly@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-10-09 01:48:12 UTC
We visited Descanso Gardens today, October 8, 2003. We were able to
find all four boxes. I wish I had brought along some plastic bags
though :-)

#1 The bags need replacing, but the contents are fine. The log book
will need to be replaced soon.

#2 We had trouble finding this one, but finally found it on our third
interpretation of the clues. This one could use new bags too. The
log book and stamp were O.K., but the message was damp.

#3 We would never have stumbled on this trail on our own. It wasn't
even on the map. That's the great thing about Letterboxing! The box
was in good shape. The bench has been turned 180 degrees to the other
side of the trail. Probably to keep people from using the extra path
:-)

#4 This box was a little damp. The call to exit had been made, so I
didn't have a chance to really check it out.

What an adventure!
