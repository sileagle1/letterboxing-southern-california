From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-02-25 19:25:28 UTC
We recently received 4 comments from some new letterboxers....
Reidling Trinity on a three box series and a single box all located
at Long Beach State college in LB, Ca. These folks have only started
letterboxing since 12/2006 but have figured out the game.

The notes to us were a small story of their day hunting all 4
letterboxes, including all of the twists and turns they experienced.
One of the series has a note in the clue text, "Only one in five
letterboxers have been able to find #3". This note only spurred them
on and they were the first in a long time to complete all of the
series in one day without asking for a hint.

While it is really easy to merely post your find and record it at AQ
or LbNA you should remember that the placers of letterboxes like feed
back on their creations. It takes a bit of time to set up a plant, so
taking a few minutes to contact the placer makes the placers time
worth while. Placers don't really expect a bunch of gushy compliments
but really like to know the finders experience.

Receiving these notes this morning made our day, and also made me
realize how sometimes Gwen and I are derelict in not contacting
placers as we should.

Don
