From: troublew_tracy (troublew_tracy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-03-03 02:13:10 UTC
Since the poll started on noon Monday, each "day" of voting after
that was from noon to noon instead of midnight to midnight-- I
figured this out on Tuesday when I kept getting told that I had
already voted, until I finally tried after noon. So if you had
voted on Thursday after noon, then that was within the last voting
day.
