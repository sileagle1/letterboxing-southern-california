Hello All --

I am in charge of helping organize kids activities for the San Diego
gathering, and I have recently been e-mailing people who have RSVP'ed
for the event and who signed up as a party of 3 or more. But I
certainly might have missed somebody, and I know not everybody checks
Atlas Quest, so forgive me for posting identical information here.....

I am trying to get a sense of how many kids there will be so that we
will have enough materials and so that the activities planned match
the ages of the kids who are coming. If you are bringing kids along,
and haven't already responded to an AQ mail from me, could you please
AQ-mail me telling me how many children will be accompanying you, and
their ages? Thanks in advance for the help!

Just for your information, here is what the kids' activities are so far:
- Outdoor toys for unstructured play (large playground balls, foam
rockets, flying disks, etc.)
- The Chocolate Game (I asked Lock Wench for the instructions!)
- Make-your-own t-shirts with a Cootie theme, or otherwise (I'll be
providing paints and some fabric stamps, all you need to do is BRING A
BLANK T-SHIRT TO DECORATE!!!!! The color doesn't matter whatever you
wish. But I will only be supplying fabric paint NOT t-shirts!!!)
(And for that matter, if you are an interested adult, you too can make
a cootie t-shirt -- this particular activity has no age restrictions!)
- Some Kids-Only boxes
- Cooties!

I look forward to meeting folks in Vista!
- Sitzmak
