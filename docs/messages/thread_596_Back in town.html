We are back and lookin forward to San Diego.

Many of the new letterboxers may not know that there will be some
special folks in attendance. If you check out the reservations you will
come across 'Pete and Wanda'. These folks are from Rhode Island, but
have traveled in many states planting a few but really their expertise
is FINDING. If you want to get blown away just ask Wanda what her
current F count is. Maybe there could be a contest to see who could get
closest to their current F number. Of course you would have to think
above 10,000.

I posted a note on the AQ California board, but thought I would post
something here as well. One of the things I plan on doing is showing
how to make a smaller version of the #1 speedball blade. I call it a #
1/2. If you want to leave the gathering with a #1/2 then you should
bring a #1 so that it can be squeezed down.
