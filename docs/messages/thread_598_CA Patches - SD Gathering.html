From: troublew_tracy (troublew_tracy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-03-23 02:32:06 UTC
To those who've expressed an interest in purchasing patches from me at
the gathering this weekend, I have some disappointing news. The
patches haven't arrived yet. I was given the impression by the sales
person I spoke with that they would get here within 14 days of when I
placed my order, but that deadline has passed and they're still not
here. So unless they show up tomorrow, I won't have them to give out
at the gathering.

I will still be taking orders at the gathering. I'll bring a box of
envelopes and all you'll have to do is pay me by cash or check ($2 per
patch), write your name on one of the envelopes, and I will take care
of the postage and send your patches to you as soon as they come.

I'm really sorry about this, and very disappointed myself.

Tdyans

Good new, everyone!
The patches are here! And will be ready to hand out at the
gathering tomorrow!

In fact, they were sent exactly when I was told they would be, and
who knows how long they've been here. When I got home this
afternoon, I decided to go check our rental office just in case they
had been delivered there. I didn't really put much hope in the
prospect, because normally when they do deliver something to the
office, they leave a note on our door telling us so. Well, either
they didn't leave the note or it got blown away or lost somehow or
other, because the package was right there in the office just
waiting for me!

See you all, tomorrow!
