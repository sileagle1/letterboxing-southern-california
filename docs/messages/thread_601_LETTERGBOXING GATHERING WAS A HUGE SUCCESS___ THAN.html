Hello Everyone,

WOW! What an amazing day and turnout for the event!!! I just wanted
to thank all of you who were able to come out for making such a
lovely day out of the event! You are all such wonderful, generous,
and creative folks! I had a great time meeting everybody, exchanging
stories, and seeing people's stamps and logbooks! It looked like
everyone had a really fun time. All the smiling faces really made me
happy, and made the time and effort in planning this event more than
worth it!!

Perhaps we can do this again in another couple of years! Of course,
at the rate this sport has been growing, we'll probably have about
500 people, and I'll need to go rent out the convention center. HAHA!

Some folks have been asking about the event boxes. I will be posting
the clues to the GG boxes on LBNA and AQ later today so you can log
them. I'll also post the pics I took online and will send out a link
to the attendees.

So, THANKS AGAIN TO ALL OF YOU!!! It was really nice getting to know
other LB enthusiasts, and putting faces to the trailnames! Hope to
see you on the trails soon!! HAPPY TRAILS!!

Always,
GG
