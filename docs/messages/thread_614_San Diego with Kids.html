From: Lyda Mae Young (lydamae27@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-03 13:09:46 UTC-07:00
We just planted the "Winnie the Pooh's Search for Owl" letterbox and have a 4 year old son who is a big fan of Winnie the Pooh.
It is in Tecolote Canyon and can be done with the Owl series (Who's Hoo?).
It is fun for kids because there is a rope swing at the destination.
It is an easy hike - but there are no bathroom facilities at this location (as is the case with several of the Tecolote Canyon hikes).

T.L.C. (The Young Family)
