From: Mary Saethre (msaethre2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-20 15:54:10 UTC-07:00
Hi LadyD!

A must see on the way to or from the Wild Animal Park
is Indians of the Acorn. The Queen Califia's Magic
Circle sculpture garden is one of a kind! General
Andres Pico is just past the WAP. We haven't been
there yet, but it is a Don & Gwen box so it has to be
special. Just off of the I-15 in Rancho Bernardo (on
the way to or from the WAP) are 3 easy boxes: Hodgee,
Painted Rocks, and Kumeyaay Indian. Vacation
Series-CA is a lot longer walk. There are several
boxes just off of I-5 near Sorrento: Mr. Natural,
Pottery Canyon, Windandsea, and A Long Shot. Near
the Torrey Pines Golf course is San Diego Sails (have
to pay a fee to park). Are you coming down or going
home on I-5? From the southbound side of I-5 are USMC
#1 and Pacific Coast Highway Series #1 and on the
northbound are Batiquitos Lagoon and I-5 Hippy Love
Van. There are also all of the boxes in Vista off of
Hwy 78 that were planted during the gathering in
March. Closer to downtown and off of I-5 and Hwy 52
there are 8 boxes in Clairemont Mesa in Tecolote
Canyon that you can get in a morning if you start
early: the 6 in Who's who series, Winnie the Poohs's
Search for Owl and How many licks?

If you need anymore, there are still a few almost
drive-bys.

MaryB (the M in RMN SD
