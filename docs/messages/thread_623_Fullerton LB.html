Re: Fullerton LB

From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-05-31 06:08:05 UTC
Hi David

You might want to recheck LbNA as there is a 3 box series listed in
Fullerton. In case you are going directly to "Search for Boxes", you
should instead just click on the book on the home page, then click on
California, then click on the bottom of the Ca. map. That will bring up
a listing of letterboxes in Orange Co, and San Diego Co.
Just scroll down to Fullerton.

LbNA and AQ are the two sites that host most of the letterbox clues.
There are other clues hidden in various spots and there are some that
are WOM (word of mouth).

Since you are new to letterboxing I would suggest that you check some
of the tutorials, FAQ sheets, and Code of Conduct listed at LbNA, Atlas
Quest, and Letterbox info. I have posted links to these sites.

Don

Letterboxing.org:
http://www.letterboxing.org/faq/faq.html

Silent Doug's site:
http://www.letterboxing.info/
Doug probably has the best info site on Hitchhikers and links to
regional chatlists.

AtlasQuest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/
Ryan has a link set up "Code of Conduct" that is really good for all
boxers to read

newboxers' group:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/
Where there is no dumb question, and is "the" place for many
newletterboxers.
