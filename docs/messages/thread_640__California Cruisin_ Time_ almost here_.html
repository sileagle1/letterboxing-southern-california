"California Cruisin' Time" almost here!

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-01-18 05:59:59 UTC
Greetings SoCal Letterboxers!

Recently Sierra Sally suggested that it might be a good idea to mention that our "Loveboat
Letterboxing Cruise III" is coming up very soon - Irene and l will be leaving from CT and RI
in just a bit over a week! It seems that all those incredibly sweet deals (starting at $179)
for suites aboard the "Monarch of the Seas" got snapped up long ago by non-letterboxers
(all "Sold Out"!), but there's still a possibility we could meet up with a few letterboxers
along the way, if anyone is available for a "mini-meet"!

I'll have the "Loveboat Letterboxing Cruise" event stamp with me for the duration of our
trip, so anyone who can catch us anywhere along the line is welcome to stamp in! Probably
the first such opportunity (other than if anyone happened to be up in Ventura over the
weekend) would be at the San Diego B St. Pier, as we get off the ship around 8AM on
Tuesday, Jan. 29th, and head off in search of "Euterpe". We would certainly love it if any
area letterboxers could come take us under their wings and help us get to a few other
letterboxes around town, but if that doesn't happen, well, we'll just do touristy things!

The following days we also get off the cruise ship at 8AM in Avalon and Ensenada, but
probably the best time to catch us would be as we disembark back at San Pedro on Friday
morning, Feb. 1st, or anytime that day actually, since we're not flying back to New England
until early the next morning. Again, left to our own devices, we'd probably just do a few
touristy things, like taking a bus to the Getty Center, Hollywood, or downtown, but if
anyone cares to join us, or help us get to any of the greater LA area "letterboxing spots",
we'd be absolutely delighted! Just let us know, swap cell phone numbers, and hopefully
we can get together! We do hope to find at least as many letterboxes on this West Coast
"letterboxing cruise" as we did on our two previous East Coast "letterboxing cruises"
(which wasn't very many - just about half a dozen while cruising and another dozen or so
before and after!), so any help anyone out there can give us will be really appreciated,
especially seeing as we're probably going to be feeling like "fish out of water", and it's
always good to see a few friendly letterboxing faces in unfamiliar places!

Cheers & thanks,

Wanda from RI ( and Irene from CT, about to embark on her first trip to CA ever!!!)
