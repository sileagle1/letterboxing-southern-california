California Cruisin' Rocks!

From: wandaandpete (wandaandpete@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-02-07 02:15:05 UTC
Just had to say what a great time Irene and I had on our recent trip out from RI to CA! lt's
taking me several days to get my "land legs" back, so I'm not going to write much now, but
reading our recently posted clues for the boxes we were able to plant while out there might
give some idea of our trip. Irene is putting together a lovely scrapbook incorporating both
the touristy things and letterboxing we got to do along the way. Best of all was getting to
meet up with Mary and Nico for some fine letterboxing in sunny San Diego on Tuesday
afternoon, and having a wonderful time with Don and Gwen in the Long Beach area after we
got off the ship on Friday! Thanks so much, guys, for helping to make our trip extra special!

Cheers,
Wanda and Irene
