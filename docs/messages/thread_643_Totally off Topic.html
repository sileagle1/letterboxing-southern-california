Many of you may be aware that I'm a Shotgun shooting instructor and do
some part time instruction at BBB range in Whittier.

(This is not a solicitation for more clients)

There is going to be an Instructors Certification course April 18,
19,and 20th and the "wanta be" instructors need practice students that
have never had any instruction in shooting.

If you or anyone you know would like to become a practice student for
this class you can call Jim Porter at 714 271 0112.

You would have to pay for your own ammunition and targets.

This is a good opportunity for novices and anyone thinking about
learning about gun safety and how to shoot a shotgun.

Don
