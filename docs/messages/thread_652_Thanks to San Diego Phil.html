Thanks to San Diego Phil

From: grumpygrinchy (ggboxer@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-09-05 02:45:22 UTC
We were delighted to receive a report today from San Diego Phil on two
of our letterboxes in the Poway area. We hadn't heard of their fate
since last year's fires in that area. Ramona Road Runner is alive and
well. Water Works has its stamp intact but the logbook is missing and
the box is broken. We hope to get there later this year to replace the
logbook and box. If another letterboxer should beat us to it, we would
be very grateful
