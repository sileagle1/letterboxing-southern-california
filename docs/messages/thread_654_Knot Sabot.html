Welcome! I'm a bit south of you, northeast of Long Beach. I think
you'll find plenty of people in your area. Vienna has been very
actively planting boxes in that area lately. And I don't know if
Buzzard is around anymore, but check out any of his boxes that are
still active in that area.

Just when I'd thought this list had fizzled out, we've had as much
activity in the last few weeks as in the previous several months.
Nice to see faces new and old poking out. I'm guessing we have a lot
of people who just lurk without posting, just checking in once in a
while to see if anything new is here. I'm guilty of that.

So if you popped in today and read this post, don't just breeze by:
reply! Like the Who's only Horton could hear, just post "We're Here!
We're Here! We're Here!"

Even though letterboxers are stealthy and secretive by nature, we're
still a community.

Kelsung
