Honor Liberty

From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-09-12 16:08:11 UTC
I recently posted this last years travels on this HHer on the main
LbNA chatlist. It was pretty dissappointing to say the least. I'm
want to see if there is any real interest in setting her free in
SoCal.

Now for those that may not be aware of what I'm writing about you
could go to the main LbNA list and search out for message #s listed
in my last post on the main list.

In a nutshell it is a HHer that gets her current (new) location
posted on where she is left not where she has been. It was released
first on 9-11-2002 and has a 9-11-01 theme. It has it's own container
and is currently on it's second logbook.

If we get a good response on this list then I'll send her to someone.
If we don't then maybe retirement might be in order.

Let me know if anyone wants to keep her bouncing around in SoCal.

Don
