It's been a few years since I was walking in the area, but there's a
park north of Brookside on the other side of the freeway, Oak Grove
Park, near the Jet Propulsion Laboratory. There was a frisbee golf
course there. I think there would be enough room and probably not
tons of people. It's an Oak grove, so not much in the way of
manicured lawns. There's also a nice shady trail that starts at the
large east parking lot of the Lab and heads north into the Hahamongna
Watershed Park. The parking lot should be open on weekends. The
major drawback is that you would need to drive to Brookside, or the
watershed trail from Oak Grove Park, if you wanted to spread out the
boxes. Descanso Gardens is pretty close. Maybe even the Eaton Canyon
boxes would be do-able.
