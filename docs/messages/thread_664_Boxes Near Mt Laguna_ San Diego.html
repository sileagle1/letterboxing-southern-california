From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-10-03 03:31:51 UTC
If you head back to Long Beach via Hwy 78 you will pass by General
Andres Pico in San Pasqual. This is very close to the Wild Animal Park.
I think the Grumpy Grinches have a few in that area as well. The
General isn't a long hike so it might take 1 hour overall.If you
search "nearby boxes" on AQ you will find several in Ramona as well.
