Hey folks,

Maulë had posted on the AQ message boards and I wanted to notify y'all
as well. She'll be running in the Long Beach Marathon on Sunday
October 12th. We'll be down on Saturday to register her and then do
some boxing in the area. On the big day of the race you should come
out, support a fellow boxer, do some exchanges, meet Atlas the
Letterboxing dog! It should be pretty great.

This is Maulë's first run with her new group, Train4Autism
(train4autism.org). T4A allows athletes to raise money by
participating in athletic competitions and then alocate the money
raised to whichever charitie(s) they choose. It was started by a very
close friend of ours, Brian Thompson, at the beginning of this year
and is already seeing great success.

So let us know if you'll be there, if you're running or if you want to
meet up! Thanks!
