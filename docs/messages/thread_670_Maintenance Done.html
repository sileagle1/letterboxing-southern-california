From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-11-22 23:02:35 UTC
You might want to consider setting up a trip to Kernville in the
Springtime. Figure on 3 very busy letterboxing days and that excludes
all of our high country boxes (the roads may not be open to those
until late May?) I think we have somewhere in the neighborhood of 60
or so letterboxes in the area.
It is approximately 3 hours, 20 minutes, and 35 seconds from Seal
Beach without stopping to box, eat or whiz.
Right now it is great hiking weather but with the holidays everyone
is super busy.
