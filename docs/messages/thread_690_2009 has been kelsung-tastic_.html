2009 has been kelsung-tastic!

From: aaaltomare (aaaltomare@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-06 02:05:48 UTC
It seems that Maul and I can't turn around without finding one of
Kelsung's boxes. The start of a new year brought us a full logbook, a
new blank logbook and more boxes than I thought we'd get to. Most of
them, however, seem to be Kelsung's. No time for GG's belt,
unfortunately, but I do remember her leaving something somewhere else.
But the fun didn't stop there, oh no.

Today, before Maul continues on her personal journey for education
and enlightenment, and before I buckle down and find my next job, we
decided to go out and contribute our own tupperware containers. The
rubber flecks are all over our couches and picnic tables, and new
images ready to be impressed and do the impressing are waiting for
your new logbooks. Is this the end for us? of course not! Don't be
foolish, I wont' have any of that. We will trudge forth through the
battlefields of lockNlocks and dollar store erasers, on a journey that
I do not see ending.

-BuffaloTony-

PS - our dog seems oddly tired today, a little too tired if you ask
me. I'm wondering what she was up to today?
