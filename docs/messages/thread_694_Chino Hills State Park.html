From: troublew_tracy (troublew_tracy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-01-12 22:45:44 UTC
I was actually considering this as a possible gathering place before
the fires. I even e-mailed them and asked if they had a policy
regarding boxing/caching. This was the response:

"There is no law forbidding geocaching, however we do have laws that
require people to stay on trails in the water canyon preserve. The
entire park has laws preventing the destruction of vegetation which
means you can not dig or cut down any plants. With that said I think
that it is permissible for you to put caches near adjacent trails out
of the preserve areas and cover them with dead vegetation."


Tdyans
