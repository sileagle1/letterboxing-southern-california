Sterling Art

From: troublew_tracy (troublew_tracy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-01-14 00:43:05 UTC
I just wanted to plug this great store, especially since they're
current having an (If-Business-Doesn't-Get-Really-Better-Really-Soon-
We're-Probably) Going-Out-of-Business Sale: www.sterlingart.com

Fellow letterboxer Teekasue turned me on to Sterling, and it's been one
of my favorite art stores ever since. If you're like me, you could
spend hours just wandering the aisles. Their prices are quite
reasonable (and you get a 15% discount off of everything just for being
a human being!) and they have a lot of stuff you can't find anywhere
else. They're the only store I know of around here that carries
Speedball carving supplies, and not just the starter kit that you can
sometimes find at Michaels. They've got handles, blades, pink stuff,
and more. They've also got stamping and scrapbooking supplies and lots
of paper for logbooks. And lots of neat sketchbooks-- I got my last
traditional logbook and current postal logbook there.

So, if you're ever in the Irvine area, remember to check them out.
They could use the business. Oh yeah, and there's free pinball in the
back of the store!
