Help Save Atlas

From: m0llykate (mkrearick@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-16 17:25:38 UTC
Our beautiful rescued pit bull Atlas (we found her while letterboxing
in Griffith Park last summer) was recently diagnosed with a
transmissible venereal tumor (TVT). This type of tumor is rare in the
US, and the doctors think she was used as a breeder in a less
developed country before she was dumped here in Los Angeles. TVT is
highly responsive to chemotherapy, and Atlas has a great prognosis for
full recovery. Unfortunately, each weekly chemo treatment costs $250,
and we are struggling to pay for Atlas' care.

If you are able to make a donation and would like to help save the
life of this deserving dog, please email us at mkrearick@gmail.com,
and/or check out our handmade "Save Atlas" buttons at
http://www.etsy.com/view_listing.php?listing_id=19815623

We appreciate any support, no matter what the type.

Many thanks,
Maul, BuffaloTony, & Atlas the Letterboxing Dog
