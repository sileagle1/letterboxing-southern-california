*sniffle* Sorry, just mourning box 3 still being missing. The Strange Stamps
series has been our favorite to collect so far and often is the first we
suggest families with young kids go after - the location is so wonderful for
more than just boxing. The day we went, we visited the aviary, played on the
trains, spent time learning about the area's history from some of the staff,
interrupted some meeting (woops) and spent half an hour just sitting and
enjoying the peace of the dwelling next to box #1. And I loved the stamp
itself for box #3, which is why it is sad to hear it's gone. Maybe someone
loved it too much?

And the Whittier Semi-Mystery is gone too?! *sniffle* Be sure and holler
when you re-plant that one - we liked the location enough to go back and
find the box a second time.

We've gotten 1 of the Eaton Canyon boxes, Buzzard :) We just got hot, mildly
cranky and made the mistake of going on a popular day - Memorial Day.
Everyone in LA goes out on the holidays. *points at stamp in boxing
journals* But see, we made it to one of them *grin* That was the same day
another letterboxer took a tumble in Eaton Canyon, ironically enough.

We also tried for the Elyria Canyon series before the heat got to us 1/4
mile into the walk. Fun drive though to get there - rather like a Euro rally
race without the fans cheering you on.

The ones up in Arrowhead are good for these hot days the next few months.
Rock Camp was a blast to visit, even without the box, and the creativity of
hiding place for the Arrowhead Overlook... I'm still trying to figure that
one out. We're lucky to get 10 minutes alone to hide a box (maybe it is the
noise created by a mass of chatty kids and adults drawing the attention) and
that must have taken a bit of time to pull off :)

All these two name handles... hmm, maybe we'll be Gno Sis instead :) We
don't sign books that way, btw, so don't expect to see that name in your
log. We are all trying to think of good boxing names during our break from
hiding - I'm trying to keep it from being a slew of Pokemon character names
though.
