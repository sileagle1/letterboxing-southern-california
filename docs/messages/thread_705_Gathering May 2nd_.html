Gathering May 2nd!

From: troublew_tracy (troublew_tracy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-17 00:34:02 UTC
It's here! http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1143

Kisma of Reidling Trinity, Lydia of Take A Hike Club and I (Tdyans)
are putting our heads together for another gathering. See details at
the link, sign up, and let me know if you have any questions.

Also, many of you have expressed an interest in helping out. Here are
a few ways you could contribute:

*On April 18th (weather permitting), I'm hoping to get several people
together to plant boxes for the gathering. There are lots of trails
in this park, and we want to cover them all, so if we get a good
group, we can split up into smaller groups and each cover a certain
area. AQ-mail me if you want to help with this.

*If you have any supplies that might help-- no-cootie stickers, boxes,
western-style decorations, a canopy, etc.-- let us know.

*If you have items you'd like to donate for a giveaway/raffle, please
contact Take A Hike Club on AQ.

*As you can see, we want to get our "planned" activities out of the
way by noon so everyone can have the majority of the day to letterbox
and socialize, so we don't need a ton of activities. But if you've
got a class you'd like to teach or anything like that, let me know.

Most of all, you can make this a great gathering by just coming, of
course! And if there's anything I've forgotten or if you have any
questions, please ask.
