Alphabet Soup

From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-04-14 02:46:17 UTC
I've been challenging and frustrating people with this complex puzzle for the last four years. So if you've spent any time working on gathering the pieces, you may want to hurry.

I'm not giving anything away by letting you know that the box is located inside a business. And the business may be closing. I'm not certain that it is, and I've been assured by some staff that it isn't, but there have been persistent rumors swirling for a while now. It is certainly a very real possibility, as the box was placed there partly in honor of a previous box that I retired when a similar business closed.

I hope that this box will remain viable for years to come, but just to be fair to those who may have already invested some time into it, I thought it important to let you know it may not. I have often doled out hints over the years as to where to look for some of the twenty-six pieces of the clue (one is just a click away as you read this). I intend to be even more liberal with my assistance from now on, and you may wish to hit up any of the finders for help, too. I state right in the clue that I encourage the sharing of information for this box.

http://letterboxing.kelsung.com/AlphabetSoup.html
