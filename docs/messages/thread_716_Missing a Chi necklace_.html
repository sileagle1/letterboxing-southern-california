Missing a Chi necklace?

From: bumblebun (bumblebun@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-03-30 02:29:43 UTC
Did someone lose a "chi" necklace about 4 or 5 years ago in Eaton Canyon?

We spent an enjoyable afternoon today boxing in Altadena -- Millard Falls and Eaton Canyon. While at Eaton Canyon, we looked for "Other Canyon Series" #4, since it was also nearby.On the streambed where one would stamp box #4, there was a necklace that said "Chi, Life, Energy" or something to that effect, with a chinese character. Since no one had found that box for 4 or 5 years, I sort of doubt it was a letterboxer ... but stranger things have happened.

We left the necklace in the box (which hopefully does not make it a geocache!)

It was kind of neat finding a box that has been in place for 5+ years, but that no one has found in a long long time. I wasn't really expecting to find the boxes there at all -- which makes it even cooler to find them. Same thing happened at Descanso Gardens a few weeks back. In both instances, these were really nice stamps, in great locations, that people seem to have forgotten.

Also, as an aside, it is surprising the number of store bought signature stamps that I see in some of the older logbooks (circa 2004)...
