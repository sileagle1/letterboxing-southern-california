Hello! Just sticking my head above water again. Although facing some serious personal financial issues of late, I promise to longer be absent from the internet. A certain fried hard-drive was to blame there, and the loss of a few bonus box clues was nothing as compared to my iTunes library!! (Back-up! Back-up! Back-up, you fool!!)

Having missed the Round Up and a planned trip to Reno, I got on tonight to learn I just missed a mini-meet in Seal Beach as well. Alas!

Thinking I would need a bit of catching up to do out in Irvine Park, my LB agenda has also lengthened thanks to a certain yellow-tinted lizard invading the parks in my local neighborhoods, and I look forward to discovering his handiwork.

Beyond that, I have some maintenance to attend to on several boxes before I plan to dive into some new plants. But I posted here in reply to Don, for he is correct to guess that, in spite of my absence from a computer, my mind has been spinning some new webs. It may be some bit of time yet, but several insidious new clues are coming.

To give a bit of a teaser, I'd actually already conceived of a clue I intended to call "Prism" when, coincidentally, Laserium has recently reopened in Hollywood, providing a perfect locale. For those who play card games, keep an eye out for "Bridge", for the spiritual I still hope (though it will be difficult) to plant "Matthew 7:7", and code-breakers will be asked to look to "The Heavens" as well as a box I intend to title simply "$".

And will there be a bonus mystery connected with these mind-numbing puzzles? Of course not. I never create bonus boxes.

Kelsung
