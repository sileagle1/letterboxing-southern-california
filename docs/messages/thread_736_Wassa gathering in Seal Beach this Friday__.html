Friends, I've been dragging myself through a few eateries in Seal Beach and have come up with 2 workable places.
O'Malleys on Main Street (100 block). A pub with liquor ,bangers and mash and lots of conventional choices. $12-18 range.
The other place is at the foot of First Street in Seal Beach and very much on the BEACH, called Riverbeach. It could be a bit chilly, outside and inside dining and no liquor ( I think) and in the same price range as above. They have a nice variety of chow, from Mexican to American. Pay parking and very casual atmosphere

I made reservations at O'Malleys for 7:PM Friday night but I'm not locked into either spot. Riverbeach would not take reservations but said their dinner time service is not too busy.

SOOOOO, I would like your imput, Riverbeach with sunset view or O'Malleys with booze. I'll post directions on Friday morning to the restaurant that gets the most response.
Regards....Gwen
