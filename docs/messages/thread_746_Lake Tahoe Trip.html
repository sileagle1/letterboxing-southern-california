Lake Tahoe Trip

From: Heather (hlynns@roadrunner.com) | Date: 2009-07-14 07:25:04 UTC-07:00
We spent a wonderful week at Lake Tahoe, and surprise there's a letterbox at the camp where we stayed. (and 2 bears in 2 days)
The box wasn't very far at all, just our usual trip to the restroom.
We were able to get hubby brother involved with his family, they got all excited and are now trying to figure out there LB trail name.
We were able to get something like 6 boxes while there. Tons of muggles so it took a long time to get it done. With 10 peaple it took all day long. Trying to teach them to be stealthy, explain about buying or making stamps, books etc.
But it was a wonderful vacation, something we haven't done in years.

BTW I saw Dons stamp in the book!!!!!Pretty cool (Sorry am stumped on Dons wifes name, I know they work the books together, if I'm totally off base just let me know)

We were also in Virginia City and Carson City NV. But did not have time to get any boxes there sadly.

Heather
