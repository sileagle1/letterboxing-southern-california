As far as we knew at the time, there were absolutely no LBs in the canyon, so I didn't have our stamp or logbook along. Found a couple of good spots to hide boxes, but haven't had a chance to go back up there to plant them.

Problem is, that area is likely almost as hot as PS, and that's a long drive, but the hike to the box from parking isn't more than a half-mile. There's a box or two up in Rose Valley, a few miles farther up the 33, but that'll also be very warm. saying that, i still have gone boxing in flip flops and lived to regret that decision. ranting about it after such a bad experience is one thing, but geeze- hiking in the hollywood hills in the summer with 3 small bottles of water? whatever!!
