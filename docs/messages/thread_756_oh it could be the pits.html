Went and check on the box today and no wonder it wasn't obvious! Someone took it upon themselves to REPLACE THE ENTIRE BOX.

"Planted originally by BuffaloTony. Replaced by Whatwhat after dropping original in the pits. Sorry, Whatwhat."

Needless to say, i'm pretty peeved, and that's putting it nicely. GIVE ME A BREAK. You've got to excuse me, but I'm venting. That box went through a lot, many people risked their hides by crawling around when the original slipped off the bridge and this guy comes along and just lets the thing go, just like that. GAAAHHH.
