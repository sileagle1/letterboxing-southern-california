From: Curtis & Paula H. (8hands4jesus@dslextreme.com) | Date: 2009-07-31 16:40:41 UTC
I would say South Carlsbad State Beach, but you have to book that seven months in advance.

Next best (and easier) in my experience, would be to book a Kamping Kabin at the KOA in Chula Vista. http://www.koa.com/where/ca/05112/photogallery/1.htm

You bring your sleeping bags, cooler, cooking necessities -- but the cabin is there, reasonably priced, electricity and lights provided, and there's a communal kitchen across the little road between cabins, with 5-6 stoves and sinks.

What I DON'T know is if Atlas would be allowed to go with you, since I haven't attempted taking our dog to the KOA.

My two cents... have fun!
