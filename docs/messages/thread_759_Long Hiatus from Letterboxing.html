From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2009-08-07 15:38:46 UTC
Wendy
My reference to "old school" was not meant as anything bad. I think if you go through many of the recent posts on the main list you will find very few "adventure reports".
That part of letterboxing has changed and folks report on their adventures very seldom these days.
I think that is unfortunate, so if anyone gives you any grief on the main list please realize that this SoCal list is a friendlier place and you can post all of your adventures here.

I think some of the other changes to our game will surprise you, some you'll like and some you may not like.

While many folks use Letterboxing.org, there are some that only use Atlas Quest (Ryan's baby). At AQ there are all sorts of bells and whistles and you have choices to use or not.

At the end of all of these changes there are still clues to follow and hopefully a letterbox at the end of your journey.

It is really good to have you back in the community.

Don

Good Morning Don,

I would never think that you would be referencing me in any way but a positive... I didn't think Old School meant anything bad, I just didn't know HOW different things had become on the List.
