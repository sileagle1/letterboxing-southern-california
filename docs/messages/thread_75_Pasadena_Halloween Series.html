Don and Gwen,
First of all, a very humble apology for my mistake about the name
John Woodson--this is completely wrong--I must have been tired out
after a long day of clue hiding. The correct name to look for in
clue #6 of my Halloween Series is James Brooks (not even close to
John Woodson). I apologize for sending you off on a wild goose
chase. If you're going to be in Pasadena on a weekend day in the
future, give me a call and I'd be glad to accompany you through the
series, or just to meet for coffee. Contact me off group for my phone
number. Orange Grove is one of the main streets in Pasadena, and
there is an exit for it going east or west on the 210 freeway. If
you were traveling east on the 210, you'd have gone through the city
of Glendale, then Eagle Rock, then you'd have seen the Pasadena
bridge to your right paralleling the freeway--the exit off the 210
near the east end of the bridge is Orange Grove.
--Buzzard

Space Holly,
You're absolutely right, and this explains why Don & Gwen couldn't
find the Orange Grove offramp from the 210 Freeway. The 210 Freeway
merges with the 134 Freeway one exit east of Orange Grove Boulevard
at Fair Oaks Avenue. The 134 ends here and the 210 takes over, but
Orange Grove Boulevard is actually off the 134 Freeway one exit west
of the interchange, not off the 210! These two freeways come
together at this spot, and will likely cause a lot of confusion for
letterboxers unfamiliar with Los Angeles freeways (heck, I'm real
familiar with L.A. freeways, and I missed this). I will rewrite my
clues saying the exit is off the 134.
--Buzzard
