Batiquitos Lagoon, Carlsbad

From: sandymess (sandymess@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-06 04:08:39 UTC
Hello Everyone,
I just returned from a 3 day visit to Carlsbad. What beautiful
weather and blue skies after the fires. I took a hike over to the
Batiquitos Lagoon and found the letterbox there. The lagoon was
gorgeous and the egrets and sandpipers were out in abundance.
Also found the "Moving a Giraffe is a TALL order" HH. So will be
placing near here in the future. Purple Iris
