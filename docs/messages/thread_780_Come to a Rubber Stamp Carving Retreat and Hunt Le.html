From: wendygault (wendygault@earthlink.net) | Date: 2009-11-04 20:13:05 UTC
Hi Everyone,

Just wanted to let you know that it is not too late if you would like to come to a rubber stamp carving retreat, Stampfest 2009. This will be year 18 for me and I wouldn't miss it. Stampfest is led by Kat Okamoto the owner of A Stamp In The Hand and The Original Rubberstamp Convention.

Spending a long weekend surrounded by friendly creative people is rejuvenating and fun. This event is open to all levels of carvers, beginners to experts, and a great opportunity to learn new techniques and share your favorite creative techniques.

Casa de Maria is an amazing location in Montecito (just South of Santa Barbara), picturesque and close to the beach. The food is wonderfully fresh and delicious.

In addition to our yearly Carved Rubberstamp Art Swap (everyone who participates goes home with a stack of hand created postcard sized prints), there are short seminars on beautiful techniques and fun projects with materials provided. There will be a series (I think it's almost 10 boxes) of temporary letterboxes (just for the weekend) available to hunt too. Great fun and all located "on campus".

When you arrive on Friday afternoon, Nov. 20, you get your own workspace, half of an 8 foot table, to set up with all your tools and supplies for the weekend. We all pack-up and leave by Noon on Monday, Nov. 23.

If you have any questions or would like further information please contact Kat Okamoto at (310) 721-9492.

I hope you can make it!!
