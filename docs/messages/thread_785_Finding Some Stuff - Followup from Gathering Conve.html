Here is the information for finding a few things that some of us talked about at the Gathering.

BRILLIANCE STAMP PADS

The Original Rubber Stamp Convention
Some of you asked me where I got my Brilliance Stamp Pads... well this is one place that I usually find them. Of course there are many other fun supplies (if all you want is carving material, I have rarely found it here) and cool techniques and lots of cool stuff that you can use for your LTCs. There are Make and Takes, and a variety of mini-lectures.

For those of you who don't carve and are always on the lookout for stamps that relate to the place you want to plant... well this is a great place to look for cool art stamps.

Info:
The Carson Community Center, 3 Plaza Drive, Carson CA
(the civic center at the corner of Carson and Avalon, right off the 405)
Nov 14 & 15, Saturday 9am-4pm Sunday 10am-3pm
$6 for one day $8 for two-day pass

OR Scramper store - 1309 Satori Ave Torrance CA

LOCK N LOCK BOXES (maybe cheaper)
CARVING MATERIAL - Plastic Erasers

Marukai Stores, at various locations (These are close-out stores so supplies come and go, if you see something you need buy it, or it may not be there when you come back)

98 cent Marukai - 1360 W Artesia Blvd, Gardena, CA‎

99 cent Marukai - 22850 Hawthorne Blvd, Torrance, CA‎
