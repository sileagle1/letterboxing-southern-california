Map of Griffith Park

From: piper_paws (hgraziano@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2009-11-13 23:27:58 UTC
I know I told some of you about my encounter with a city coucilmember at Griffith Park while I was out planting boxes, but for the rest of you, here's the story:

On the Thursday before the gathering, my husband and I were leaving Fern Canyon Nature Trail and saw a foursome of inappropriately attired people entering the trail. I'm talking, super high heels, tight skirts, business suits. Well, they kind of accosted us in that way that only a true politician can. The long and short of it is, they asked us if we had a map of Griffith Park. I pulled out our copy of the map I included with the clue packets and said, we had this, but it wasn't that great. Well, Mr. Politician says "We have a great map. We'll send you one." I got the email address of one of the women and sent her my information.

I got the map in the mail today and it is a great map! I suggest that anyone who wants a great map of Griffith Park to contact Councilmember Tom LaBonge's office and request a copy of the Map & Guide of Griffith Park.

www.lacity.org/council/cd4
tel: 213-485-3337

The map was created by Cartifact. You can see the cover of the Griffith Park map and a little piece of it on their website.

http://cartifact.com/printmaps/index.shtml

-Piper Paws
