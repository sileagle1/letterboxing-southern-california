From: alarch2 (alarch2@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-11-26 21:07:45 UTC
Box 1 of the series has been temporarily removed due to animals eating its protective shrubbery. Said shrubs have thrived there for years, but the wildfires last summer destroyed much habitat in the foothills, and that may be causing the wildlife to forage more aggressively in places which weren't burned. The other three boxes in the gardens are still in place.
