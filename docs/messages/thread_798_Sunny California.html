I have started researching the boxes in the area, hoping to find some that would entice them to find another and another and another.... From: alarch2 (alarch2@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-12-13 21:26:08 UTC
Welcome! You have good timing - there are several newish plants in the Torrance/PV area. I haven't found them so I can't make specific recommendations. One great historic box is Don and Gwen's Point Vicente Lighthouse box which was planted in 2001. Looking through the logbook is practically a history lesson. = )

We had a gathering in Griffith Park last month so there are over 30 new boxes there! If your family aren't into hiking, several of the boxes in the Fern Dell area are more accessible. If they're hikers, there are some very cool series on the north, "zoo" side of the park. (Griffith Park actually has three different "access" points that are not connected by public roads in the park. The third area, Bronson Canyon, has the original, 1960's Batcave and a letterbox commemorating it, as well as more boxes.)

There's a lot of variety in So Cal, so hopefully you can find some boxes in places your relatives want to visit, anyway.
