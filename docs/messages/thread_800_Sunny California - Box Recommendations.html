From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-12-15 09:01:58 UTC
> Since I probably can't fit in all the boxes, I would love to hear about any particular favorites you may know of that would help with my endeavor to introduce my family to my newest obsession, Letterboxing!

Just my two cents, but here's what I would recommend, from "gotta have" to "probably check out":

Point Vicente Lighthouse - The oldest box in the area and a great view!
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=3152

Moonlight Rider - Cool stamp and recent Box Of The Week winner!
http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/clue/?gBoxId=127847

Lanfill Looop Trail Series - Nice easy hike.
http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/clue/index.html?gBoxId=102053

Peaceful Garden - Nicest box of the several in this park.
http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/clue/index.html?gBoxId=101891

Reservoir Dogs - Not too shy to toot my own horn.
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=44957

Little Whale Watch - Many locals don't even know about this one.
http://alum.wpi.edu/~p_miner/California.html#Box_220

Da Blimp! - Get a box and maybe take a ride!
http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/clue/index.html?gBoxId=90410

Pirates Treasure - #2 is also at Point Vicente.
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=23768

Andy's Box - Dedicated to a fallen soldier.
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=45730

South Coast Botanical Letterbox - Only one in the area I don't have.
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=124329

Marsh Preserve - Nice place, but probably pretty wet right now.
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=44962

C Over D - Cute and easy.
http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/clue/index.html?gBoxId=108529

The Shipwreck Trail - Might be missing, but probably just hard to find.
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=3155

The Palos Verdes Peninsula Picture Puzzle - I just planted this one, and after doing all these others, you just may have covered enough ground to figure this one out.
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=51715

That's a good selection. Anyone want to add or subtract from that list?
Kelsung

PS I just wish the Abalone Cove boxes, my San Pedro Series, and the Manhattan Beach Pier were still active. Alas.
