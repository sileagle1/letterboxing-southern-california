Re: Venice Letterboxes?

From: Alarch (alarch2@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-01-08 20:51:25 UTC

Golden Gecko had a couple of boxes that were accessible by water only. Unfortunately, she recently pulled all her boxes and clues. Hopefully someone else knows something.

Kayaking is a lot of fun! My only other suggestion is if you plan to visit a box, don't take your entire journal. =-) Just paper to stamp on, and glue it into your book when you're back on dry land. Kayaks sit low in the water so they tend to get a bit sloshy.
Have a good time!
