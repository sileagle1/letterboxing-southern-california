Chino Hills Update

From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-02-21 08:30:27 UTC
Today I went from worried to excited. Thanks to a hot week, the park was officially open today, and even if we do get more rain, it should be a lot lighter and only cause 48 hour closings.

So I not only got 8 boxes planted, but I prepared the locations for 9 more. That is, I lugged rocks to spots that didn't have them to make a SPOR where I needed it. I've hiked so much already now, I may spend the gathering just resting. ;)

If I carve the rest I'm planning and receive the others I've been assured of, I should have four overlapping loops that include 14 boxes, and another loop of 6 more. Beyond that we will have 8 each from Lee and Mr.Ospital, so that's 36 new boxes where there once was none.

Kelsung
