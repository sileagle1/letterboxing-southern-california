South Coast Botanical Letterbox Update

From: Adam (clarinet_226@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-02-22 02:52:05 UTC
This is a message to all who have not found my South Coast Botanical Letterbox. The clue says that there is a fee, and there is. But if go on the third Tuesday of any month, it is free to all ages. This would be the best time to go if you already haven't. These gardens are really nice and I hope with this news about the free day, that this box will have more finds, even though its a store bought stamp, but it fits with the area it is planted.
