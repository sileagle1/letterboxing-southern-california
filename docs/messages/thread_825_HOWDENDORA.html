It's time we had an updated SUBJECT line for this discussion, yes?

Whew! Thanks be to GOD for protecting us from our little friend. I guess our little guy [California Newt or Taricha torosa] was set free before he was stressed enough to interpret our visit as an "attack". If we had come at him, say, with the sharp beak of a bird, his response would have been decidedly less calm. I guess he saves his toxins for the serious stuff -- after all, we didn't hurt him at all, just delayed him in wherever he was going... probably looking for a mate, based on the article I read.

http://animaldiversity.ummz.umich.edu/site/accounts/information/Taricha_torosa.html

I used to keep these guys as pets, when I was a kid and found an occasional one in my backyard. Never had any problems with them.

I DID touch the poison oak with my fingertip, but took out a "wipe" right away and cleansed my finger... and since no rash has developed yet, I think I must be in the clear. Laundered all our clothes, too, since our jeans had come into contact with the poison oak, too.

Hmmm... I wonder now if I should do something for my hiking boots. Any suggestions???

And yes, Zuhl is Cool, I did remember to "empty" my backpack. :)

Additionally... Kelsung kept his promise and set our event up on AQ (about midnight)... please sign up, all who haven't done so yet. Since it's now past, perhaps you're having difficulty finding it? Here's the link: http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1642

I was able to find it because I have a FAVORITE SEARCH set up for gatherings in my area... and that's what I click on, anytime I want to find it.

Blessings,

Hansenclan
