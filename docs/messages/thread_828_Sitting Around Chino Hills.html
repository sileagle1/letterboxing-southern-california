Kelsung, what a great event. Thank you for organizing it and prevaling against all of the natural forces against you. I still can't believe how much hiking you did to plant all those boxes! That was some serious one man dedication.

I'm glad such a good group was able to come with it being postponed. It was great to see everyone.

Also, for being billed as a hiking event, I was surprised at how many boxes I actually got. Twelve! Thanks to Lee for planting a series of "waddle" friendly boxes and to my personal chauffeur for the morning, Kelsung, who hinted where a couple of other boxes might be located. And of course, my favorite box of the day "Piper Pause." How awesome to have my very own dedicated Lee & not so much Nancy box. I have cut out the clues and pasted them in my log book next to my find. That made my day :-)

Oh, and for Dezert Ratty, here is the song I heard as my son was going to bed... "I love my rat, I love my rat, Don't take it away from me." He slept with him Saturday night and then took him to the park with us on Sunday.

After the baby comes, I'll have to plan a little meet and greet. :-)

-Piper Paws
