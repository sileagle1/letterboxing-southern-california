Don & Gwen,
Ventura County is definitely not Southern California (I think of it
as the Central Coast), but let's get back to Tiburcio Vasquez.
Vasquez is Southern California's most famous bandito; obviously he
roamed far afield, but one of his most famous escapes was up the
Arroyo Seco while being chased by a Sheriff's posse, through present-
day Pasadena. If you're going to plant a Vasquez series up in Kern
or Ventura County, I'm going to have to plant a sister series in Los
Angeles County, so this needs to be a trans-county series.
--Buzzard
