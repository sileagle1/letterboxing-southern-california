Discovering Covina's Parks: Bonus Box - temporarily out of commission

From: WaterLossGuy (8hands4jesus@dslextreme.com) | Date: 2010-05-13 04:34:26 UTC
Tonight, while my daughter's Girl Scout troop was planting plants as a service project for the park, I sauntered over to check on my bonus box that was in the park. Alas!

Someone "discovered" the box indeed -- and its contents were strewn all over. The good news: I have my stamp! The bad news: The logbook is gone. I have the other, less important things: the box itself, the baggies, the paper towel the stamp was wrapped in.

Really, I'm thankful to have the opportunity to mostly just "put it back together" rather than just have it missing and having to start all over. I can recreate the logbook, and even give credit to those who logged their finds at AQ and LBNA. And... here's my opportunity to switch to a better quality box. I'd figured a bonus box wouldn't get much activity, so wouldn't need a higher quality box, but might as well have it secure.

Makes you kinda wonder what they did with the logbook, doesn't it? I wonder if someone will end up contacting me about it, one of these days.

Just sharing my experience, not really upset or anything.
(Probably because I still have my stamp!)

Have a nice evening/day...

Hansenclan
