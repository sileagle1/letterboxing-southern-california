I also wanted to thank you all for your plants. While I wished I could have, I didn't get to all of them. It seems to be quite a challenge to plant in CA as you do not have very many overgrown trees and such as we do where I live in parks and cemeteries.

Walt (My most prized stamp being a HUGE Disney fan)
OCTO
Lair of the Beast
Heritage House
Crystal Catherdral
Find Nemo
Apollo 1 (We found 3 of 4 boxes)

I was hoping to make my first plant near Yorba cemetery, but could not find the perfect spot. I vow to be back though and 2 of the 4 people I converted to letterboxing have plans to plant at UCI and UCLA.

I am so sorry that I was unable to meet with you all (Kelsung had asked if he could arrange something) but we were so busy all weekend. I hope to come back for more fun and hopefully we can meet and box together.
