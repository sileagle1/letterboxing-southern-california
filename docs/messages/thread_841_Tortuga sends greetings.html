From: Kim Flood (fldnpdls@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-05-25 23:04:43 UTC-07:00
Gave Tortuga, Charmin and Running Wolf (two of Ryan'sPCT pals)a big carb loaded spaghetti dinner tonight.
He apologizes for missing Lee's boxes, and was upset that he had done so!
Evidently he printed off his clues, on AQ :-P, a couple weeks prior to his start date, and hasn't checked since.
Due to the PCT closure and detour, he walked right past one of mine and RAV's too, and he had the clues to RAV's!!
Anyway, I handed the three of themoff to a friend of mine, that lives pretty close to where I picked them up this evening, and after a shower -MUCH NEEDED- and hopefully a good night's sleep, they will be off on their adventures bright and early.
They should beat Vasquez Rocks by Friday I would imagine. I think they said they are going about 12 miles a day??
I totally forgot to ask him about the 2 and if he knew what portions are closed? Sorry Kelsung:-(
Dezert Ratty

P.S. (I have memories of spending a couple of Thanksgivings, as a kid, at Vazquez Rocks, with a couple of other families. Now that I'm the one who cooks for Thanksgiving, I realize what a lot of work and preparation it must've taken, for the moms to prepare and then pack up our Thanksgiving meals, so that we could all picnic there at Vazquez Rocks. I did check last week, to see if there were any letterboxes there, but alas... there weren't. Then again, who wants to reach into a crevice where there might be a rattlesnake? And... what's to prevent kids who are just playing from reaching in and finding letterboxes? Then again, all I remember are the rocks... and me out there, making believe I was "Charlie the Lonesome Couger," of Disney movie fame.)
