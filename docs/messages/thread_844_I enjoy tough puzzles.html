From: leenancytanney (luckielee@aol.com) | Date: 2010-06-17 05:11:04 UTC
Sometimes when you write these puzzlers, and know the answer; it seem so obvious. I deliberately did not add much of a hint, to keep it challenging, but plan to add a new hint to the clue every month it goes unfound. So you might want to revisit the clue each 22nd of the month (probably not at that same time each month - is this a hint? Of course not - it not time yet.) Nancy says nobody will ever get it, but I know better, by the impossible clues on some of Kelsung plants; and how quickly my "POG" and "Citrus" boxes were found.

We are honored and suprised Kelsung found 77 of our boxes. That's well over half of our 145 plants, 26 are outside of California, 3 in Northern Cal, and many were retired fairly early, not to mention the 5 that have never been found. "SE Lamppost LP" being one of them, and no reported finds on the Nimrod series in Kernville, CA.

Happy Hunting,
Lee & Nancy

I honestly love the brevity of that clue, and once I've found it (and I WILL find it) I'm sure I'll praise its cleverness to other boxers, as I have with POG, Citrus and Geometric Oddity (which, I recall, I convinced you to make even a few words shorter after I found it).
