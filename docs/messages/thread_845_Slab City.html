Salton Sea - what could you possibly be doing out there?

hmmmmmmmmmm, I wonder if that's why I don't care for fish too much?

hi there! 
a "mae west" was the slang for the old type life jackets - because back when they first came out, Mae West was the "it" girl of the day.
The vests are really bulky, especially around the "chest" area.
I was soooooo skinny as a kid that with a life vest on, it was a struggle to stay up right! 
I'm glad someone got smart and made them a little more stream lined :-P
