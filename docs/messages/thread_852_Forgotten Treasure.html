Forgotten Treasure

From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-07-13 03:36:04 UTC
Alright, who's up for a real hunt with me?

As some of you know, I've made it my mission to confirm or deny the deaths of many of the older boxes in the So Cal area. Among others, I have successfully found two of Buzzard's Repetto Ranch Raid series, and two of Q's Idiot Box series.

Well, I have a new goal, and wonder if any would like to join me. Way back in Jan 2007 I went after The Data Recovery Mission. I found box 1, but not 2 and 3. At the time, 4 and 5 were listed as missing, so I didn't bother going any further. I was still young and weak.

Q has since retired the listing, and the note in the clues says Unknown as of 5/11/07, however Granite Pioneer logged finds on boxes 4, 5 and 7 in Nov 2007. I think at least those three, if not others, may still be viable. They are at least worth a look.

So, maybe we can plan a day to get together at Bronson Canyon. Who's in?

My ulterior motive is that Buffalo Tony, Maule and I planted a box up this same trail in 2009 that has since gone missing. Since I want to get a replacement out there, this is an excuse to motivate me.

Kelsung

PS My real Holy Grail currently is Buzzard's Marvin box, the clue to which was in Commander K-9, which is missing. My only leads are Lee (who says he once possessed the clue, but never hunted the box) and Alarch (who may have found the box). I think the three of us need to pool our resources (i.e. your fuzzy memories and my undaunted determination) and hunt this one down.
