The following is from Miss Chievous and was posted on AQ under LB Exchanges;
I'm going to visit my home town for the first time in 15 years in September and I want to plant a few boxes while I'm there. The town I grew up in is at the base of an awesome canyon in the Los Angeles Crest National Forest and no plans at all, yet not that far from other places where people have planted in the L.A. county area. This canyon is very beautiful and versatile, and HUGE. Fishing, camping, hiking, great place to dump a body, are all wonderful options of this canyon. Ah, you were paying attention! I'd like to plant a series that would be a murder mystery of sorts. Every box would represent a fictional person who fell victim to a serial killer on the loose who needs to be stopped now! Through this hunt you will need to find clues and evidence that will lead you to solve the mystery and get this guy off the streets!

I can provide the logbooks and containers. Also in the area is the house from E.T., the end of Route 66, a really old pop shop that carries all sorts of sodas and not one comes from independent small companies, my high school is pretty famous claiming it's the most filmed high school in the world, and a local landmark the cross of San Ysidro which is like our towns Hollywood sign.

Any interest please AQmail me!
