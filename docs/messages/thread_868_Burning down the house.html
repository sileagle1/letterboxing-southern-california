Burning down the house

From: Kim Flood (fldnpdls@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-10-13 14:06:23 UTC-07:00
I'm sitting here at my desk, trying to wade through a few hundred e-mails - and I notice a stack of papers has burn marks on them?? What??
There is a window to my left, and the stack of papers is on a file cabinet pretty level with the window - I don't smoke, I don't have candles here . . . then it dawns on me - somehow my light-up magnifying glass (attached to my desk should I ever find time to carve - ha ha) has gotten turned around and was in such a position that the sun was coming through it, at one point in the afternoon, and doing the "death ray" thing on those papers!
It actually got hot enough to burn the edge of one of the papers about six inches!
Scary thing is - we were gone all weekend, including Monday, to AZ :-O
Almost homeless Dezert Ratty
