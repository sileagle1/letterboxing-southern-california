From: Deanna (ethanksmom@aol.com) | Date: 2010-10-17 00:59:06 UTC
the kids & I might actually be back... Bella finally chose a trailname a while back (Ding Dong Bell), drew a bell, and I carved it, but because our newest boxer is still kinda young (2 1/2), it's been hard to get back out there. We did scout a Lee & Nancy box, but got interrupted by a local (good thing I had such a great homeschooling science lesson cover story), and I'm itching to go back & get it. Archer Boy and I scouted a couple of local boxes a few weeks ago, and today, with Mr Deanna along to help keep a lookout, we picked them up!

Nice, quick drivebys, with good clues and nice stamps... You know the type, where you scan the area, and a voice in your head says, "it's gotta be... right... about... THERE," and it is? Sweet!

So if you see my Palm Tree with it's Celtic knot, or Archer Boy's bow & arrow, don't be surprised to see a little bell, with a 6 year old's "DING" written alongside. And I have to say thank you to those who have been planting, because there are a lot more to find than there was a few years ago!
