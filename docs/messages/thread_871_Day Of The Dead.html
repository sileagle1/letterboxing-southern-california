Years ago, some of you may have been lucky enough to find the "Calavera Catrina" box before it came up missing. Sadly, I did not. The stamp was amazing, and so was the history lesson and location.

The planter was Aljan Sundance from Colorado. She inquired of me a while back about planting and adopting a replacement. As of this weekend, that has taken place. Once she posts the clues, I encourage all of you to check out "Sweet Candied Death" in East Los Angeles. It is a really cool stamp with a handmade logbook.

And it's in a beautiful old graveyard: Go in the sunny afternoon.

Kelsung
