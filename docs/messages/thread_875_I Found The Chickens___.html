I Found The Chickens!!!

From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-11-19 02:35:11 UTC
Anybody who has checked out my "Freeway Chickens" letterbox would know that it was disappointing both for me as a planter and you as finders to not actually find any of the legendary chickens.

Well today I was in that area for my job, so I stopped by and checked up on the box. But when I went to get back on the freeway, I had to take a different entrance to get to my work destination that I'd never taken before. And, Lo and Behold, that's where they were! It's stupid just how excited I got over this discovery!

Needless to say, I'll soon be moving my box a few blocks and updating the clues!

Kelsung
