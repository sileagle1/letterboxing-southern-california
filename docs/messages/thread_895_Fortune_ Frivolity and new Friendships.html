From: lifetish (jd@studiopi.com) | Date: 2011-04-10 05:02:46 UTC
Thank you Kelsung for sponsoring an amazing day for me!
Thank you not only for the best game of KLUE I have ever played (and not won), but for the wonderful artwork I will have in my log book to fondly remember it all. Such a generous and contagious spirit you are!

I loved that everyone had the passionate spirit and assertive command of leaders and yet, everyone got along (not so common with so many leaders, eh?).

I can't believe it has taken me this long to attend an exchange and i am aware that I chose a special one for my first. Team Peacock, Team Scarlet, Team White, Team Green (congrats again)and Team Plum - how lovely to share the adventure with you all.

And to my team Mustard - thank you for adopting me into your clan so warmly. What a wonderful family you all are!

I hope everyone else traveled home as safely as I did. I look forward to seeing you all again sooner than later (if only to share my stamp with the Peacocks I left empty-stamped).

And i might just start posting my finds. Thank you all again!

Lifetish (pronounced Life Fetish)
