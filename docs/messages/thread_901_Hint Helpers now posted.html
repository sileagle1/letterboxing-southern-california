Hint Helpers now posted

From: leenancytanney (luckielee@aol.com) | Date: 2011-05-21 07:06:33 UTC
After planting my "SE Lamppost LP" a year ago with only a single finder, I thought I had better give some real hints or it will never be found again. Obviously it was too hard, and the monthly hints too abstract, so I have posted Hint Helpers to explain the weird hints.

Keeping with that in mind, my "Verbose" box has gone unfound for close to five months, So I have added a Hint Helper to that clue as well. Although still abstract and you must have the slip of paper from me with the hint to find, it does "sort of" help you decipher that hint; and "sort of" leads you to another abstract hint to the boxes.

Happy Hunting,
Lee & Nancy

(actually Nancy takes no blame for these abstract puzzles)
