What in thee heck??

From: Kim Flood (fldnpdls@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-08-13 00:18:05 UTC-07:00
So last night I can't sleep, as usual, and I decide to clear up some work on my computer - finally get pooped out and turned off all the lights in my "office".
I grabbed the telephone in one hand and my glass of water in the other and headed through the dark to the light switch on the landing.
It's not that far and even though it was pitch dark I was going along pretty good - ran smack into my black dog, who had decided to lay directly in the middle of everythingand flew about a two feet up in the air and came smack down on both knee caps.
Now, if I was a gazelle that might not have been an issue, but being an out of shape overweight getting older woman - OUCH!! OUCH!!
Good news is - I did not land on the dog and only spilled a few drops of water - go figure.
My husband wakes up and wants to know what I am doing on all fours in the dark - the dog wakes up and scurries off with it's ears burning from the littany of curses being flung at it . . . . I have rug burns on both knees and I can't get up - AND that's not the weird part . . . . .
After finally getting up and realizing I didn't break anything ( I hope) and getting to sleep - I start to dream.
I dreamed that some new Letterboxers out my way (who do actually exist) misunderstood how Letterboxing works and had "adopted" each of my plants by moving them and then re-listing them as their own??
It was a really weird dream, that got into more detail than I remember - but what in thee heck?
All that from tripping over a dog and damn near killing myself :-o
I was thinking it meant I had repressed anxiety over being too busy to box or check up on my boxes??

FYI - Found a package of 4 Lock&Lock's at Ross for $5.49. They are 0.3QT (350ml/11oz). I normally pay $2.50 each, and have to buy6 or so for that price -plus shipping, so thought this was a pretty good deal.

Hope everyone is well and having a great summer - sweet dreams
Dezert Ratty

PS: woke up with my left knee so stiff I am walking stranger than normal :-(
