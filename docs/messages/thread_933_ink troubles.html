Re: ink troubles

From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-09-02 02:36:50 UTC
Yes, Michaels has ink dots in the same isle as their other ink pads, along with or near the rubber stamps. They are what I used for Kelsung Klue.

They come in packs of four, with three different variations: if I remember correctly, one has red, blue, green, and black; one has purple, light green, light red, and yellow; one has gold, silver, metallic brown and metallic green. I find that the metallic pack seems to dry out more quickly, but the others I'll have in my bag for a year.

Kelsung
