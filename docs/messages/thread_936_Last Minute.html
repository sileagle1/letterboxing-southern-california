From: jsbain (jsbain@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-10-22 05:07:48 UTC
The new "Santa Monica Place" ( the outdoor mall at the end of the 3rd st promenade) has some good dining options. There is "The Market", which is sort of gourmet fast food (lots of little independent shops), a couple restaurants and a surprisingly nice food court.

Tender Greens in culver city is good, though a little on the pricey side, and sometimes pretty crowded so might not be good if a bunch of people end up coming.
