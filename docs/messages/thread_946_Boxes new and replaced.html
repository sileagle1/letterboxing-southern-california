Boxes new and replaced

From: Kelsung (kelsung42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-02-03 05:13:34 UTC
I know this is blatantly self-serving, so excuse my huge ego. But in the last half of 2011 I slacked off not just on finding, but also on planting and maintaining my boxes. So as of Christmas, I said No More! In just the last month I have checked up on the majority of my plants, retired some, replaced several, and planted a few new ones.

When you plant a new one, people notice. But if one went missing a while ago, people tend to forget or ignore the listing after a while, so I figured if I didn't hold up a big neon sign, most of you wouldn't notice, so that's why I'm posting this. The following boxes have all been recently replaced:

San Pedro Series (all three of these are now just small magnetic containers, but I HAD to replace them because I LOVE the locations and still want others to discover them.)
Penn & Teller
Skyknight
X (for anyone who has solved my PT)
Sound Guy (actually a new box in a whole new location)
Rose Hills
Prism (along with Lee's Big Dogs #1)
$
Seeking Skinny (actually just moved another box)
Space Shuttle
Alliteration (also in a whole new location)

Along the way, Dezert Ratty also confirmed that Duel was still in place.

The only one left to replace is McDonald's History #2: Speedee, which I'll be getting back out soon. Meanwhile, I finally planted #3: Golden Arches, along with new box Treasure Island, and I planted Juicy Fruit by I dig toasters to replace her Spearmint box.

So what's next on my agenda? I can't tell you, but I can give you a Klue....

Kelsung
