Letterboxing at Girl Scout Camp

From: Bob Prager (pragerr@alumni.princeton.edu) | Date: 2003-11-25 06:03:15 UTC
Had the opportunity to expose about 50 adult Girl Scout Volunteers
to letterboxing over the November 14-16 weekend. Unfortunately, the
boxes are on private property, so I don't think they count for the
challenge, but we'll get more letterboxers out of this one! They
were all surprised at the number already in place in the Long Beach
area (thanks to Don and Gwen -- and Chowder -- and...)

If you'd like to see what it looked like, I put the information up at

http://www.geocities.com/pragerr/SkylandLetterboxing/letterboxskyland
.html

and

http://www.geocities.com/pragerr/SkylandLetterboxing/stampmaking.html

"Bob-baloo"
