The Hunt for the Borg Cube

From: jim54invc (chaosmanor47@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-06-28 05:44:27 UTC
I can't find anything now, but I have this vague recollection of reading someone wondering if this venerable cache was still there.

Let there be no wondering: it's in great shape, high in the hills north of Claremont. I was out that way hiking with a friend, and I had the clues for this along with me. He had found this LB some years ago, by accident, but we used the clues, as he couldn't remember much about it. Aside from missing one clue (easily rectified), and having to take a guess at one point, due to an "extra" path that probably wasn't there when the box was hidden, this was highly enjoyable and easily followed. Very creative clues, too. And there are two log books: the original is full, while the second one has plenty of room.

It was quite warm, so we didn't do nearly as much hiking as we had planned. This area will be very hot and dry for the next three months, so extra caution is required. Heed any red-flag notices. Bring extra water. Consider going out very early in the morning, or late in the afternoon. But if you have been putting off going after this because you weren't sure if it was there, you can put those worries aside :-)

Jim, the other half of chaosmanor
