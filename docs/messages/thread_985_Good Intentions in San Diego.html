Heads up to those who have letterboxers in the San Diego area. The local Girl Scout troop, has taken it upon themselves to do maintenace to the area's letterboxes. Here is the notification I received this week.

Lee

________________________________

Hello,

Our girl scout troop is currently working on our Silver Award project. To complete this project we are restoring letterboxes in the San Diego community. We recently searched for the Jack Murphy letterbox. We are please to inform you that so far we have searched for 9 letterboxes all over San Diego and yours was the only we found. that being said, however, we did restore it by adding an ink pad, pen, and paper. We hope our efforts help you and our community become a nicer place. We cannot update our findings on the website but hope that you will use this information to update the webpage. Thank you.

Sincerely,
Troop 3277
